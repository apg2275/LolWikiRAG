    • Janna similarly references her old voiceover in her own joke.
Captain Gangplank
  • ▶️  "They can pay in gold... or blood." resembles ▶️  "I always take my toll, blood or gold."
  • ▶️  "Prepare to be boarded!" references his very first pick line: ▶️  "Prepare to be boarded!"
  • ▶️  "What shall we do with a drunken sailor?" references the eponymous sea shanty.
  • In the Turkish localization, ▶️  "A messy death is your first warning." is replaced with "Racon kesmem, kafa keserim." ("I don't act tough, I cut heads."), which references the TV series Kurtlar Vadisi.

    • "Não aguenta? Bebe leite." ("Can't stand up? Drink milk.")
    • "É tu mesmo, seu fanfarrão!" ("It's you, your bragger!")
    • "Aí! A casa caiu! A casa caiu!" ("Hey! Things fell apart! Things fell apart!")
    • "Véi, na boa, volta para casa!" ("Bro, come back home!")
    • "Vou sentar o dedo!" ("I will pull the trigger!!")
    • "Vambora! Não mandei ninguém parar!" ("Let's go! I didn't tell anyone to stop!")
    • "Vamo invadir pelo mato." ("Let's invade through the bush.")
    • "Eu sou o novo xerife." ("I'm the new chief.")
