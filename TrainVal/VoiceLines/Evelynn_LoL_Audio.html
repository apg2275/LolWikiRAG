Trivia[]

Evelynn, Agony's Embrace
  • ▶️  "Misery loves whatever I tell it to." alludes to former quote and popular refrain ▶️  "Misery loves company.".

  • ▶️  "Without pain, how could they know pleasure?" is likely a spin on a quote from John Green's novel The Fault In Our Stars: "Without pain, how could we know joy?"
Coven Evelynn
  • ▶️  "I only live deliciously." is similar to ▶️  " Dark child. Wouldst thou like to live deliciously?", which in turn references The Witch (2015 film).
Evelynn, the Widowmaker
  • ▶️  "All too easy." quotes The Empire Strikes Back.
  • ▶️  "I may be bad, but I feel good." quotes Army of Darkness.
