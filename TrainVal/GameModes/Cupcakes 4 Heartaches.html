Cupcakes 4 Heartaches was a crafting metagame in League of Legends where Caitlyn bakes cupcakes with the help of Amumu and Vi. It was released as a prototype exclusive to the OCE server on February 10th, 2023 and ended on February 21th, 2023.[1]

Trivia[]

  • Cupcakes 4 Heartaches was only released on the OCE server in order to test new technology within the metagame. OCE was chosen due to being an English-speaking server that was large enough to validate assumptions without the need to engage with a larger population.[2]
  • The codename for the metagame was called Caitlyn's Charity Bake.
