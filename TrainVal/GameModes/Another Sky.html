League of Legends

Another Sky was a visual novel in League of Legends telling the story of a new generation of Star Guardians preparing to fight back the forces of chaos. It was released on July 14th, 2022 (1:00 PM PT) and ended on August 24th, 2022 (11:59 PT).[1] Due to on-going issues with reward distribution, the entire story and all rewards were unlocked on August 12th, 2022.[2]

Game

Another Sky

By Jared Rosen, Taylor Dinwiddie, Ty Sheedlo

As a new generation of Star Guardians prepares to fight back the forces of chaos, rising star Akali finds herself wracked with doubt. Does she have what it takes to be a Star Guardian? Or will the darkness she senses inside spell doom for her and her friends?

Starring: Akali, Ekko, Fiddlesticks, Kai'Sa, Morgana, Nilah, Quinn, Rell, Sona, Syndra, Taliyah, Zoe

Mentioned: Ahri, Akshan, Ezreal, Gwen, Jinx, Lux, Miss Fortune, Rakan, Xayah

Chapter 1

Act 1: Another Sky

One year after Zoe's attack on Valoran City, Kai'Sa now leads a new team of Star Guardians consisting of Ekko, Sona, Taliyah, and her best friend and co-captain, Akali. She begins to experience visions about Zoe's attack and wonders if they can protect the city now that the other guardians have left. As they spend their last day of spring vacation training, Akali expresses her concerns to Kai'Sa, worrying about their futures as guardians. Akali wonders how long they will have to be guardians, if this is all they are meant to do, but she is interrupted as the other guardians begin to goof off and they end the training session. Kai'Sa and Akali continue to talk at the park, where Akali looks to her friend for guidance, but she too is uncertain of their fate. However, Kai'Sa reassures her that they will face anything together, as both Star Guardians and best friends.

Act 2: Star Nemesis

As the school year begins, Fiddlesticks begins its hunt for Akali, preying on her growing doubt. Akali and Kai'Sa walk home after a long day of school, Akali jokes about using her Star Guardian powers to attack their strict teacher, Professor Puboe, but Kai'Sa reminds her to be responsible with her powers. She also points out that Akali has been skipping their training sessions and chides her about her responsibilities as a Star Guardian, but Akali lashes out. She retorts that Kai'Sa has changed since they became Star Guardians, only caring about the mission and how they no longer spend time together as friends. Kai'Sa apologizes to her friend and confides that she feels the need to be more responsible ever since she began experiencing visions of Zoe's attack. Akali reveals she has been seeing and hearing things as well, thinking about the casualties of Zoe's invasion. She worries that being a Star Guardian means feeling like this all the time. Akali also believes that something has been following her and they soon hear an eerie voice. As they begin to leave, the street around them begins to distort and the two Star Guardians are ambushed by Fiddlesticks. Fiddlesticks' aura of fear prevents Akali from using her powers, but Kai'Sa manages to transform and drives it away. As the corruption begins to fade, they are approached by an unknown figure. She begins to antagonize the guardians, commenting on how the guardians are shackled to order of the universe. She reveals herself to be Morgana and muses around the idea of replacing their professor. Morgana then departs after telling them that Fiddlesticks is going to pay their teammates a visit and the two guardians are left shaken by the encounter.

Act 3: The First Test

Morgana appears elsewhere in the city and begins her test of the new guardians. She summons a horde of monsters and they being to wreck havoc on the city. Akali and Ekko soon arrive on the scene, but Akali is distracted by Fiddlesticks' influence. The fear of dying in battle reminds Akali of Zoe's invasion and causes her to doubt their mission even further. Kai'Sa arrives and takes command, they notice that the corruption on the streets is similar to Fiddlesticks' and sense that Morgana was here. Despite her hesistation, Akali remains committed to the mission and the three of them fight off the monsters, while Sona and Taliyah cover the entances. The guardians manage defeat the monsters and celebrate their first victory as a team. As they prepare to leave, Kai'Sa can't help but feel the monsters were defeated too easily.

Chapter 2

Act 1: Nightfall

Kai'Sa visits Lux's statue at the city center and look to her for guidance, uncertain on how to save her friends. Later at school, Morgana informs the class that she will be replacing Professor Puboe after he had an "accident" during the attack on downtown. She vents to the statue, feeling frustrated that she can't do anything as Morgana continues berate them in class. Akali soon finds her friend talking to the statue and begins to talk with her. Kai'Sa then receives a text from a stranger who wants to meet with her. Fearing that it is Fiddlesticks, she decides to deal with it alone, but Akali decides to follow her. At the outskirts of the city, the strangers are revealed to be Syndra and Zoe. Kai'Sa is shocked to find Zoe still alive after Rakan supposedly killed her and even more surprised that she is accompanying Syndra. Syndra explains that they are temporarily working together due to a mutual threat, Fiddlesticks. She reveals that Fiddlesticks has been preying on Star Guardians since the first group, her and Zoe's, and that it has grown too powerful for either of them to handle alone. She wants to work with Kai'Sa's team in order to stop it once and for all, to which Kai'Sa agrees. As they depart, Akali, who had been eavesdropping on the meeting, is attacked by Fiddlesticks.

Act 2: Remember Us

Kai'Sa tries to speak with Akali at school, but Akali is furious with her for allying them with Zoe. Kai'Sa tries to explain to Akali, but she refuses to listen, feeling as though Kai'Sa cares more about being a Star Guardian then her well-being. Akali wants Kai'Sa to reassure her that everything will be fine, but she cannot make that promise. Akali leaves angry at her friend. Kai'Sa heads to Noodle Bill's to clear her head. There she finds Syndra enjoying his ramen as well. Noodle Bill reveals that he has fed many generations of Star Guardian teams and is aware of their secret identities. Syndra reveals that the other guardian teams were quite similar to her's, having to navigate their own high school dramas and wrestling with their own fears. She also reveals that her original team consisted of her, Zoe, Akshan, 4Gwen, and Harp, but most have ended up fallen, the ultimate fate of Star Guardians. Syndra explains that Zoe believed killing and revivng guardians would allow them to void their contract with the First Star and avoid falling. As Syndra finishes her food, she tells Kai'Sa to avoid the problems they had, their failure to save their friend led to the destruction of their team. Kai'Sa ponders the information Syndra told and promises to save Akali from falling.

Act 3: Lord of Fear

Fiddlesticks begins another attack on city when it is spotted by Syndra and Zoe. They begin battling the monster while Kai'Sa and her team gather to aid them. They remain hesistant about trusting Zoe, but put aside their differences to defeat the monster. Fiddlesticks then teleports the guardians into its chaos dimensions, where it is at the height of its power. The guardians launch attack after attack, but gain no headway against it, until Akali notices a Star Guardian familiar trapped within it. The guardians then focus their attacks on the caged familiar and manage to destroy Fiddlesticks. A girl emerges from Fiddlesticks' remain, whom Zoe recognizes as Harp, her best friend. Syndra reveals that Harp was the first Star Guardian to ever fall and it resulted in her becoming Fiddlesticks. Zoe apologizes to Harp for not being able to save her, but Harp also apologizes for thinking that she could keep going. Harp begins to fade, but tells Zoe that she will always be her best friend. With the threat ended, Zoe decides to leave and promises to return, but thanks the guardians for helping her save her friend. Syndra also decides to leave, but not before informing the new guardians that an Outer Guardian will soon be arriving to mentor them. She explains that her time as a hero is over and that she is part of a much darker story that she must return to. Syndra departs as a shooting star containing Nilah appears in the sky, hoping she can train the guardians for the trials to come.

Chapter 3

Act 1: The Stars Beyond

At school, the guardians discuss the shooting star that appeared after the battle. After Fiddlesticks' defeat, they feel as though Morgana's power has increased. They also notice that Akali has been absent since their battle with Fiddlesticks. They are soon interrupted by the sound of fighting caused by two new transfer students, Quinn and Rell. The guardians introduce themselves to the transfer students when they reveal that they are Star Guardians as well. Kai'Sa welcomes them into their team and promises to introduce them to Akali later. Meanwhile, Akali continues to experience visions and is frustrated that she is the only one still suffering. Morgana soon approaches her and she transforms, ready to fight. Morgana reveals that she is not responsible for her visions and wants to offer guidance to the young guardian. Akali refuses to listen to her, but her negative emotions begin to swell and she begins to fall. Morgana stokes her descent before she is interrupted by Nilah, an Outer Guardian. She manages to halt Akali's fall and prepares to face Morgana, but Star Nemesis isn't ready to face her and departs. Nilah explains that she is here to help train their team and the two meet up with the others.

Act 2: Broken Chains

At school, Morgana continues to taunt Akali, with her aura hiding her true intent to the rest of the class. Kai'Sa tries to comfort her, but Akali continues to have doubts. She believes that even if they defeat Morgana, they will have to keep fighting stronger threats until they die. Akali wishes that she had never taken the Star Guardian oath, surprising Kai'Sa. She tries to talk her friend out of such thoughts and decides that they will spend the day tomorrow, just the two of them, without any Star Guardian talk. As Kai'Sa leaves, Morgana appears and prepares Akali for her fall.

Act 3: The Herald

Morgana unleashes a Dark Herald that rampages across the city as she and Akali look on. The other Star Guardians soon arrive to stop it. Kai'Sa asks Nilah on how to beat it, but the Outer Guardian defers the strategy to the captain, stating she is here to teach them, not do their jobs for them. Kai'Sa devises a plan to stop the Herald and the Star Guardians triumph. At Morgana's palace, Morgana notes that despite its defeat the darkness unleashed by the herald was more than enough. With its power she plans to usher in a new age without light, darkness, and Star Guardians.

Chapter 4

Act 1: Fare Thee Well

Kai'Sa confronts Morgana about Akali's disappearance, but Morgana continues to taunt her. She later finds her friend at Lux's statue, where the two of them first met all those years ago. Kai'Sa begs her to rejoin the guardians, but Akali refuses. Akali is fed up with the burdens of being a Star Guardian, either destined to die in battle or fall from the light. She lashes out against her friend and states that she is joining Morgana and runs off.

Act 2: Everything Ends

Morgana declares to the Star Guardians that it is almost time for their final battle and that they had better prepare. Kai'Sa makes her final rounds checking in on her teammates. At Arcade World, Ekko and Sona spend their remaining time playing Valorant. At Cafe Cuties, Quinn, Rell, and Taliyah bond over eating pastries. Kai'Sa then meets with Nilah who reveals a final surprise: Akali.

Act 3: Star Guardians

Morgana begins her endgame and unleashes multiple Dark Heralds against the city. The Star Guardians gather to face them when Kai'Sa reveals that Akali is still on their side. The other guardians question her loyalty, but reluctantly trust her and head into battle. As they leave Kai'Sa and Akali alone, Akali begins attacking her best friend and unleashes her fallen powers. As she prepares to strike her down, Morgana intervenes and brings them to her palace, wanting to see Kai'Sa die in front of her. As Morgana eagerly awaits the captain's demise, Akali betrays her, revealing she was buying time for the rest of the guardians to arrive. The rest of their team soon arrives and they charge Morgana. The Star Nemesis gains the upper hand at first and shackles the guardians, but Akali and Kai'Sa's bond allows them to harness full power of the First Star and frees their team. The guardians then combine their attacks and destroy Morgana. Later at the winter festival, Kai'Sa apologize to Akali and she forgives her. They are then met by Ahri who tells them to speak with Xayah. As she begins walking to her, Kai'Sa experiences another vision. She sees a massive Dark Star, who warns that despite defeating its agent, none will stop it and it shall consume all.

Epilogue

Act 1: Another Sky (Redux)

In the aftermath of the invasion, Zoe manages to remanifest herself, but notices that her powers have diminished. She is approached by Syndra, who mocks her fall from power. Syndra notes that she can finish her off, but she knows that Zoe know that, having realized what she has become. When Zoe asks why she hasn't done so already, Syndra notes it wasn't her fault that she became the way she did, but the First Star's. Zoe questions if Syndra intends to team up with her to take it down, but Syndra states her goals are far different. However, she proposes one last mission together, to put Harp to rest, intriguing Zoe.

Act 2: The Twilight Star

Zoe continues to think about Harp's last words at the winter festival. She ponders destroying the city again before being interrupted by Rell. Rell talks with Zoe, seemingly unaware that she is the Twilight Star. As they continue their conversation, Zoe begins to calm after losing her best friend and eventually leaves, thanking Rell and promising to kill her last when she returns.

Act 3: The Dark Soverign

Syndra visits Nilah at her galaxy. Nilah explains that she is Outer Guardian, entrusted to protect their universe from outer threats. Syndra experiences a vision from the Dark Star asking her to break chains, giving her a headache. When Nilah goes to check on her, Syndra retaliates and she senses a dark power within her eye patch. Sensing a worthy foe, Nilah prepares to fight, despite Syndra's reluctance. Syndra drops her façade, hoping to gain some answers if she played along. She reveals that she has found something that could break the cycle for Star Guardians and has big plans this universe. Syndra then takes off her eye patch and unleashes her power against Nilah.

Starlight[]

  • The events of Another Sky plays out over a series of chapters, each three acts long.
  • To progress through a chapter, you'll have to defeat two "bosses" by generating Starlight—45 Starlight for the mid-boss, and 90 Starlight for the final boss. Chapters unlock every week up through Chapter 4, and you must complete one chapter before progressing to the next.
  • Earn Starlight by play matchmade games of Summoner's Rift (except Co-op vs. AI), ARAM, TFT, or Ultimate Spellbook to gain starlight. Starlight progresses each chapter of the story
Objective Starlight
Play a game of Summoners Rift 10
Win a game of Summoners Rift 5
Play a game of ARAM, TFT or Ultimate Spellbook 6
Win a game of ARAM or Ultimate Spellbook 3
Place 4th or higher in TFT 3
Play as a Champion from the Star Guardian Universe 3
Play on a team with 1 / 2 / 3+ Star Guardian skins 2 / 3 / 4

Star Guardian Bonds[]

Another Sky isn't just an opportunity to see the latest chapter in the Star Guardians saga—it's a chance to learn more about each Star Guardian. Visiting the Guardian Stories section of the Another Sky page will allow you to see your unlocked Star Guardians along with their Bond levels. You'll immediately be able to click any of the available champions to see their introduction story, but if you want more than that, you'll have to forge a closer Bond.

Bonds for Akali, Ekko, Kai'Sa, Sona, and Taliyah unlocked on July 14th, while bonds for Nilah, Quinn, and Rell unlocked on July 28th.

Act 1: The Rogue
Akali and Kai'Sa are enjoying a meal at Noodle Bill's. As Akali scarfs down a bowl of noodles, Kai'Sa reminds her that they have training later, but Akali brushes her off. They are then interrupted by Noodle Bill, who tells them a story of how he makes ramen broth. He normally reads the newspaper as it simmers, but one day he decides to take a stroll instead. He ended up walking for hours until he remembered his broth was still cooking, he eventually found his way back stall and found that his broth was still intact. Seeing the Star Guardians confused, he explains that the moral of the story is trust in one's friend. Friendship is like a good broth, you need to put in the time, effort, love to make it good and that even if you aren't there, it will simmer on. As Akali leaves Kai'Sa to pay for their meal, Kai'Sa confides to Bill that she hasn't been as good a friend to her lately. Bill tells her things won't get easier, but they have to trust one another, reassuring her.

Act 2: Promises
Kai'Sa heads to the park for Akali, who requested an "Emergency Star Guardian Meeting." After a waiting a bit, she decides to seek out her friend to see why she needed her. She decides to head to Noodle Bill's and finds her after she came back for her leftovers. She confronts her about the so-called emergency, but Akali brushes it off. Kai'Sa rants how it feels like Akali doesn't seem to care about her responsibilities, but Akali retorts that Kai'Sa seems to want to lead Lux or Ahri's teams instead, that she's chasing after someone else's standards. Kai'Sa asks what was the emergency that Akali wanted to talk to her about and she reveals that she has been having dreams about the Battle of the Twilight Star. When she reaches for her weapon, she can't find it, instead she's in her school uniform and she hears the sound of the screams and begins to fall. She tells her that that its easier for her imagine falling instead of being a Star Guardian forever. Kai'Sa tells Akali she doesn't have to be a Star Guardian like the others and Akali responds that neither does she. Akali wants to forget this conversation and leaves with her food.

Act 3: Forever and Ever
Kai'Sa continues to think about the last conversation she had with Akali and how poorly she handled it. She waits at Noodle Bill's hoping to catch her. Bill reveals that he talks with her when she is eating there and that she often talks about the uncertainty of her future. Kai'Sa feels ashamed that her best friend feels like can no longer confide in her. Bill notes that they each have their own burdens to carry, but if they carry them together, she'll come around. Kai'Sa eventually finds Akali at Lux's statue. She confides in her that part of her wishes that things could be like they were before the invasion and while their lives can never be the same, the old Kai'Sa, her friend, is still here. The two then make up and promise to always be there for each other.

Act 1: The Boy Who Shattered Time
Kai'Sa watches as Ekko use his time travel powers in class, concerned that he is misusing his powers, she plans to confront him after class. When she catches up to him, he tells her he can't hang out after school due to a project. Having not asked him to hang, she realizes they have already had this conversation due to him rewinding time. She chides him to use his powers more responsibly, instead of using it to get out uncomfortable situations. Ekko makes a comment that they can't change anything when it truly matters and heads home to work on his mysterious project. Intrigued, Kai'Sa decides to follow him home and spies on his project until he finds her with his powers.

Act 2: Rewind
A few weeks later, Ekko continues working on his project and decides to invite Kai'Sa over to see it. He reveals to her the ChronoDisplacer, a device that amplifies his temporal powers from the First Star and allows him to go further back in time. He explains that he plans on using the device to change the past, now that he's stronger, and wants Kai'Sa's help to operate the device, but she remains hesistant. He reveals that during Zoe's invasion, his friend was put into a coma and crippled trying to save someone during the chaos, now he wants to go back in time and warn him. Kai'Sa reluctantly decides to help him and they prepare the device.

Act 3: Moving Forward
After several failed attempts, Ekko and Kai'Sa manage travel back one year to Zoe's invasion. They transform into their Star Guardians and head out to find past Ekko. They manage track him down and see him talking to his friend, Claggor. As past Ekko heads towards safety, Claggor stays behind to find his friend, Sona, who is paralyzed by the overwhelming sound. He runs into Kai'Sa and Ekko, confused as to why his friend came back dressed in cosplay. As Ekko tries to warn him to leave, but Claggor realizes that they are from the future and that Ekko has found a way to time travel. Despite his excitement, Claggor wonders why Ekko traveled here of all places and they reveal that Claggor ends up getting hurt trying to save someone. Claggor appreciates his friend's sentiment, but decides he must help his friend, even if it hurts him. Ekko reluctantly accepts his friend's decision and lets him leave. Kai'Sa and Ekko then return to current time and he thanks her for the help.

Act 1: Daughter of the Stars
Akali and the other Star Guardians plan on taking a field trip, while Kai'Sa stays behind to protect the city. Kai'Sa must also look after everyone's familiars while they are away. As Kai'Sa tries to get the familiars to train, Umi, Akali's familiar, sneaks away to have their own fun.

Act 2: Space Babies
Kai'Sa and the other familiars begin their search for Umi. They start their search at the school, at Chiizu's suggestion, thinking Umi would want to learn, but find it empty due to the field trip. Next they search Cafe Cuties, where Waku tries to convince Kai'Sa to buy the "Special Cutie Surprise Special Menu Doughnut with Bonus Smooch". Then they search Arcade World at Aru's suggestion, where Akali usually spends her day gaming. They eventually find Umi at Noodle Bill's. Bill manages to convince Kai'Sa to take a break and she enjoys a nice bowl of his ramen.

Act 3: The Old Familiar Feeling
The next morning, Kai'Sa realizes she needs to work on her work/life/Star Guardian balance and decides to take it easy for the day. She and the familiars debate on how to spend the day and eventually decide on a day at the park. Relaxing at the park makes Kai'Sa notice she hasn't spent much time relaxing with Akali since they became Star Guardians. She realizes she has lost herself a bit since taking the oath, but she wistfully hopes it will end, some day.

Act 1: Joy Unbound
Nilah arrives in Valoran City and meets with Ahri and Miss Fortune. She has been tasked by Lux with training the new Star Guardians, but catches wind of a fallen Star Guardian, however, Ahri and Miss Fortune assure her they will handle him. Kai'Sa meets with the Outer Guardian to learn from her experience. Nilah agrees to answer one of her questions, but the rest she must earn: either through combat or guile. She learns that as an Outer Guardian, Nilah is centuries years old and has been fighting the forces of chaos for years. They then begin their training.

Act 2: The Price of Power
Kai'Sa and Nilah spar, but Nilah overwhelms her and knocks her out. When Kai'Sa wakes up, she finds herself in her bedroom with Nilah and Akali. Nilah reveals that part of her power comes from Ashlesh, the Star of Joy. While her team proved victorious against the monster, she was tainted by the cosmic joy, but managed fight it back and now wields its power as her own. Nilah notices that Akali and Kai'Sa have a strong connection and believes it can be stronger through shared trials. She considers luring a monster to city for them to fight and bond over.

Act 3: Bliss Forever After
The Star Guardian team trains against VR monsters in Arcade World. While the others face off against Omega Gromp, Nilah takes Kai'Sa aside and shows her her personal galaxy. She reveals that this is all she has left, her past life has been lost to time and that Nilah isn't even her real name. She tells Kai'Sa that while they cannot change their path or regain what was lost, they can enjoy what they have, when they have it, and to hold her friends close, no matter what tries to tear them apart. Seeing how much Kai'Sa has grown, Nilah considers her promise to Lux kept, but decides to stick around.

Act 1: Small Town Wings
At school, Akali, Kai'Sa, and Quinn are assigned to do a presentation about their life. Kai'Sa sees this as an oppurtunity to learn more about their new teammate and invites Quinn to work on the project with them. However, Quinn invites Rell, causing Akali to storm off, wanting to work on it with just Kai'Sa. Quinn apologizes for potentially causing any issues, but Kai'Sa tells her its not her fault, as she and Akali were having some issues prior.

Act 2: The Eyes and The Heart
At Cafe Cuties, Kai'Sa, Quinn, and Rell brainstorm ideas about what to talk about. Rell soon heads over to another table to settle a debate over "Beastman Chronicles: the Pitmaster Saga". Quinn shares about her life before coming to Valoran City. She reveals that her and Rell are from a small town named Kiiso and that they didn't talk much before becoming Star Guardians. Due to being isolated from the rest of the guardians, Quinn had to train Rell, with help from the Official Star Guardian Daily Planner. Quinn admits she does miss her friends and family, but talking with Kai'Sa does make her feel better. When Rell returns, she reminds Quinn they have plans at the mall to indulge in her secret hobby and they leave, while Kai'Sa finishes the rest of Quinn's homework.

Act 3: Soar
At school, Quinn gives her presentation about her hometown, Kiiso. She shares that in Kiiso, everybody knew each other, it was a place where promises never break, and friendships last a lifetime. She was eventually transferred due to her strong academics and misses her friends and family, but is excited for the new friends and adventures to be had in Valoran City. Later at the park, Kai'Sa congratulates Quinn on her presentation. She relates that being a Star Guardian is sometimes easier then being oneself and admits that she misses how things used to be with Akali. They acknowledge the difficulties of being a Star Guardian, as only they know the struggles, but she can always talk to her if she needs. Kai'Sa asks Quinn if she could feed Valor, to which she agrees.

Act 1: The Iron Maiden
At school, Kai'Sa finds Rell beating up a bully. She intervenes before things can escalate, but Rell makes him apologize to the first years. Kai'Sa chides her to not waste time on bullies, but Rell states that as Star Guardians they have to protect the innocent. She quickly leaves, dropping one of her books, Kai'Sa picks it up and sees that its a manga named "Derelict Emperor #282". Kai'Sa finds Rell talking to Lux's statue and showing off the new Star Guardian uniforms. She then transforms back into her school uniform, but quickly leaves as people spot the transformation. Later at Cafe Cuties, Kai'Sa, Rell, and Taliyah are enjoying some snacks, however, the plot line of Cafe Cuties confuses Rell. Kai'Sa notes that it isn't any more complicated then her manga and tries to return it to her, but Rell snaps at her, denying that it belongs to her. They then spend some time at Arcade World, playing Valorant with Ekko. Kai'Sa and Rell later patrol the park, where Rell attacks at a deer for making a child drop their ice cream. After getting kicked out of the park, they head to the mall, where Kai'Sa tells Rell that her actions have consequences. Rell explains that she is used to only having to worry about herself and perhaps it is better that she be alone. Kai'Sa tries to tell her that she doesn't have to be alone as she is part of a team, but Rell vents about constantly letting people, letting slip that her parents are dissapointed in her. When Kai'Sa tries to pry more, Rell leaves, not wanting to get close to anyone again.

Act 2: Derelict Emperor
Quinn tells Kai'Sa there is only one place where Rell goes when she wants to be alone, the comic store. She finds Rell after purchasing the latest issue of Derelict Emperor, when Rell spots her, she asks to speak with her in private. Kai'Sa takes her back to her room, where Rell shows her her manga collection. She reveals that she once had an even larger collection, but her parents threw it out when they found out. Rell explains that she comes from a powerful political family and that her parents had high expectations for her, so they got rid of anything they deemed a distraction, such as her manga and friends. She eventually got fed up and decided to be her true self, causing her parents to be dissapointed in her and sending her away to Valoran City. She tries to not make any friends, except Quinn, as she knows she'll end up pushing them away. Kai'Sa reassures her that the team wants to be her friend and makes a deal to stand by her side, if Rell agrees to open up to them. Rell accepts, however, she is scared that her parents will try to scare them away, but Kai'Sa welcomes the challenge.

Act 3: Alone Together
In the following days, Kai'Sa notices Rell being more open, openly talking about manga, sharing her Star Guardian stories at Lux's statue, bonding with Ekko and Taliyah, and fighting semi-wild animals. Rell eventually asks her how it feels being the authentic Rell, to which she replies that she loves it. She thanks her for being so supportive, but wonders why her parents didn't like her true self. Kai'Sa notes that parents are complicated, but until then, their squad can her family. Rell thanks everyone for being her friend, finally feeling like her heart is full.

Act 1: Maven of the Strings
At school, Akali and Kai'Sa notice that Sona is constantly late to class. Kai'Sa decides she needs to get to know Sona better in order to improve how they work as a team. She flags her down and asks her about her tardiness, to which Sona replies in sign language. Chiizu translates, telling Kai'Sa to meet them at the mall, where they'll explain.

Act 2: Silence
Kai'Sa meets Sona at the mall early the morning. Chiizu explains that they chose the mall early in the morning because there is much less people making noise, noise that drowns out Sona's thoughts. He reveals that during Zoe's invasion, Sona lived in the district where the battle was the worst, and the sounds of destruction was defeaning. It overwhelmed her, she was paralyzed as she watched her neighborhood turn to rubble. The defeaning sound is the reason why Sona arrives to class so late, as she waits for the rush of people and the bell to stop. However, she is a Star Guardian now and feels braver when she is in the uniform. The Mall's Mascot suddenly appears and scares Sona away. As the mall begins to get busier, the sounds start to overwhelm Sona, Kai'Sa tells her to focus on one of the sounds, but it proves to difficult and she takes off.

Act 3: Our Symphony
As Kai'Sa contemplates how to help Sona, the city is attacked by a monster. Kai'Sa decides to it on with Sona, but Sona thinks they should call in the others. However, Kai'Sa reassures her that she is strong enough to defeat it, but will call them if necessary. Chiizu explains there is more to Sona's story, he reveals that during the invasion that Sona would have been crushed by rubble, if not for the bravery of a boy who pushed her out of the way. If she the power of the First Star then, maybe he wouldn't have gotten hurt while saving her. Kai'Sa tells her that even though she couldn't help him then, that doesn't mean she can't help people now and that being a Star Guardian doesn't make one brave, but being brave is what makes one a Star Guardian. The sounds of battle soon begin to overwhelm Sona, but Kai'Sa realizes that Sona shouldn't try to focus on one sound or drown them, instead she should listen to the music of the city. Sona realizes that all the sounds are part of one big symphony and begins to hear it. After defeating the monster, Kai'Sa congratulates Sona and tells her that she will be there for her, but on the days she feels overwhelmed by the sound, to take it one note at a time.

Act 1: The Starweaver
At school, Kai'Sa searches for Taliyah, needing to return Aru, her familiar that she left in class. Once she finds her, she notes that Taliyah acts different at school compared to when they are training. Taliyah tries to explain, but feels embarassed to talk about it at school, so they agree to talk after class. They then meet at Cafe Cuties, where Taliyah arrives in an old hoodie.

Act 2: Threads
Kai'Sa and Taliyah spar a bit and head to Cafe Cuties after training. Kai'Sa asks her why she seems so different at school compared to at Cafe Cuties, where she is more outgoing. Taliyah explains she doesn't like the school uniforms, so she tries to avoid being seen while in them. She hasn't found a style that suits her yet, as she has been busy rebuilding her neighbor, becoming a Star Guardian, and starting high school. However, wearing the Star Guardian uniform makes her feel like person she wants to be, so if she can find something that make her feel that all time, she can throw away the hoodie. Kai'Sa then decides to help Taliyah find something more fashionable, with Akali's help.

Act 3: A Whole New You
Akali and Kai'Sa take Taliyah shopping at the mall. Akali goes off to find more clothes for Taliyah, while her and Kai'Sa talk. Taliyah reveals the hoodie is one of the few things that she saved from her old house, but she feels that sometimes its hold her back, tying her to the past. Kai'Sa sympathizes with her, feeling haunted by the past sometimes as well, but they can figure it out together. Akali soon comes back, after stealing some clothes, and the three quickly leave before security can catch them. At Kai'Sa's bedroom, Taliyah feels more confident in her new clothes and plans on throwing out her hoodie, but wants to reveal her new outfit later. Taliyah comes back out in her school uniform, but now accepts them, realizing that she was holding herself back, not the clothes. She thanks the two for her helping her, but still plans on burning the uniform after they graduate.

Trivia[]

  • Another Sky features numerous references to both skins and base lore, and other games made by Riot Games:
    •  Cafe Cuties exists as a cafe in the Star Guardians universe, with the champions acting as mascots for the cafe.
      • Viego is mentioned to be a part of the universe.
    •  High Noon exists as a movie franchise in the Star Guardians universe, with each release with a legendary skin acting as a movie.
    •  Battle Academia,  Steel Valkyries, and  Pentakill are comic series within the Star Guardian universe.
    •  K/DA and  Dunkmaster exist in the Star Guardian universe as arcade games seen within the background of the arcade.
    •  True Damage exists in the Star Guardian universe, as confirmed when Akali sang half of the chorus of GIANTS in Chapter 4 Act 1. However, it remains unclear if it exists as a band or as another form of media in the universe.
    • A billboard showing Space Groove Samira in the background of the city center confirms the existence of  Space Groove in the Star Guardian universe.
    • The fried chicken restaurant chain Birdio works for can be seen behind the ramen cart and in the background of the mall.
      • A poster of Pizza Delivery Sivir can also be seen in the fried chicken restaurant at mall.
    • Professor Puboe's class being like a prison references the town of Puboe, where there was a prison controlled by the Order of the Shadows.
    • Noodle Bill references Oyster Bill, Nilah's landlord.
    • Arcade games for League of Legends: Wild Rift, Legends of Runeterra, Ruined King, Teamfight Tactics, Valorant, and Song of Nunu can be seen within the background of the arcade.
    • A Valorant storefront can be seen in the background of the mall.
    • Socialite Nixie and Battle Principal Yuumi can be seen together on one of the billboards in the Valoran City Center backgrounds.
  • Akshan is revealed to have been part of Syndra's original team.
  • Ekko's friend Claggor, who is featured his sidestory, references Claggor from Arcane.
    • Claggor was crippled after saving Sona from falling debris during Zoe's invasion.
  • Derelict Emperor, Rell's favorite manga, is a synonym for Ruined King, a reference to Viego.
  • In the final acts of the story, Morgana was revealed to be an agent of the  Dark Star, an entity similar to the First Star and is its nemesis.
    • The  Dark Star also seems to be trying to corrupt and/or exert its influence over Syndra, as revealed in the final act of the story's epilogue.
  • Kai'Sa's dad is Kassadin, as mentioned by Akali & Nilah in Nilah's sidestory.
  • Seraphine, Orianna, Senna, Xayah, and Rakan all make a brief cameo appearance in the Winter Festival background.
    • Rakan also makes a brief dialogue cameo in the second act of the epilogue.
  • Lux makes a brief dialogue cameo in Nilah's sidestory.
