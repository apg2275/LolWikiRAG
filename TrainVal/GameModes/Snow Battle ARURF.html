Snow Battle ARURF

Sign in to edit
  • View history
  • Talk (0)
"You belong in a museum!"
This article or section may contain obsolete information, but exists here for historical purposes.

Snow Battle ARURF is a game mode inspired by Ultra Rapid Fire, and created to commemorate  Snowdown 2017 skins.

The game mode was only available twice, over the patch V7.24 cycle:

  • 12–19 December, 2017
  • 2–9 January, 2018

Overview[]

  • Map: Summoner's Rift 5v5
  • Draft type: All Random, with limited champion pool
  • Mode: Classic

Features[]

  • All playable champions are available for all players, and most use a frost-themed skin.
  • Snow Battle buff
    • Everyone has the summoner spells Flash and Ultra Mark / Ultra Dash. Equip your poro icons!
  • Rune substitutions:
    • Unsealed Spellbook is exchanged with Kleptomancy.
    • Cosmic Insight is exchanged with Approach Velocity.
    • Ingenious Hunter is exchanged with Ravenous Hunter.
    • The Ultimate Hat is exchanged with Nullifying Orb.
    • Manaflow Band is exchanged with Nullifying Orb.
    • Presence of Mind is exchanged with Triumph.
    • Lethal Tempo is exchanged with Press the Attack.
  • Disabled items:
    • Mejai's Soulstealer
    • The Dark Seal

Poros[]

If one the following Summoner icons is equipped, the in-game poro that follows your champion will have a custom skin, and Ultra Mark will throw a poro (with the skin) instead of a snowball. Additionally, Ultra Mark's and Ultra Dash's icons will be changed to use Poro Toss' and Poro Dash's respectively.

Battlecast Poro
Gentleman Poro
Shadow Isles Poro
Astronaut Poro
Dragonslayer Poro
PROJECT: Poro
Star Guardian Poro
Dark Star Poro
Baron Poro
Reindeer Poro

Trivia[]

  • The in-game tooltip for Ultra Mark and Ultra Dash are a rewritten version of Mark and Dash.
    • Ultra Mark: It is written that you throw a snowball "approximately 1 Really Far units away". Dashing to the tagged unit, deals damage and increases "your swagger by like a million percent". Additionally, it's written: "If you have Poros following you, you throw a Poro instead of a Snowball, because we're terrible people."
    • Ultra Dash: "Ultra Dash to the unit by your snowball (or your Poro, you monster).", and also: "PUSH THIS BUTTON YOU KNOW YOU WANT TO".
