The Choncc Dome is a visual novel in League of Legends telling the story of Soul Fighter's training process with the mentoring from the Little Legend. It was released on July 20th, 2023 (1:00 PM PT) and ended on August 28th, 2023 (11:59 PT).[1]

Game

Tournament of Souls

By Taylor Dinwiddie, Ty Sheedlo

Play League of Legends to build Samira's reputation and style on your competition at the Tournament of Souls! Face off against the strongest soul fighters in a series of fierce 1v1 matches to help Samira chase down her biggest bounty yet and fulfill her SOUL'S DESIRE!

Starring: Evelynn, Gwen, Jhin, Lux, Naafiri, Pyke, Samira, Sett, Shaco, Viego

Mentioned: Isolde

Gwen / Pengu

Only a sharp edge can cut path to victory!

Part 1
  • Choncc: Gwen practices sewing training dummies back together with her soul thread. While most have been mended, none of them look the way they did when they were new.
  • Gwen: I'll break as many needles as it takes to get this right!
  • Pengu: That's the spirit! Remember what you're fighting for!
  • Gwen: My best friend. And the chance to fix my past mistakes.
Part 2
  • Choncc: In the midst of a duel, Gwen pulls out her giant scissors. Pengu responds by pulling out their sword and striking a heroic pose.
  • Pengu: You're an adventurer just like me, Gwen!
  • Gwen: With your sword and my soul thread, we can help the ones we love the most.
  • Choncc: Pengu and Gwen return to their training, both more motivated than ever before.
Part 3
  • Choncc: Inspired by Pengu's spirit, Gwen's determination grows. She dashes forward, and Pengu misses their opening to parry!
  • Pengu: Wah! My flipper!
  • Gwen: My best friend, Pyke, hurt himself, too. I tried to save him, but....
  • Gwen: This time, I won't fail! I'm going to heal you properly, Pengu!
Part 4
  • Choncc: The once-broken dummies gleam with the power of Gwen's soul thread, which has only grown because of her training.
  • Gwen: This should do the trick!
  • Choncc: Gwen heals Pengu's injured flipper! Just to be safe, she wraps it in a cast to keep Pengu from injuring it again.
  • Pengu: Wah, you did it! Thanks, Gwen!
Part 5
  • Choncc: Gwen and Pengu celebrate, and as Pengu strikes another heroic pose, their cast breaks open, showing a perfectly healed flipper!
  • Pengu: Look, Gwen! It's all healed!
  • Gwen: Before Gwen can react, a letter materializes in front of her: an Invitation to the Tournament of Souls! She's finally ready!
  • Gwen: Let's steal the show, Pengu! Whatever happens next, I get to decide!

Lux / Nimblefoot

The light of friendship unlocks the power of our souls!

Part 1
  • Choncc: Training in Chonce's Dome, Lux fires a light blast at a moving target dummy—but misses.
  • Lux: Pushing my limits, running away from home... it's all been so tough.
  • Lux: But I have to keep training. I won't let my powers dim!
  • Nimblefoot: Don't be afraid! Master your soul's gift!
Part 2
  • Choncc: As the training dummies dart around Nimblefoot and Lux, Nimblefoot uses the distraction to steal her bracelet!
  • Nimblefoot: Y'know, Lux, I'm on the run, too.
  • Lux: A sweetheart like you? No way!
  • Nimblefoot: I've always been a tricky lil' runner. Keeps me safe.
Part 3
  • Choncc: Lux, looking tired but earnest, watches as the training dummies race around the room.
  • Lux: I thought running from my family would free me. But I still feel bound.
  • Lux: Still, if I hadn't gone, I wouldn't have made a friend like you!
  • Nimblefoot: O-Oh! Aww, Lux, about that—I need to come clean...
Part 4
  • Nimblefoot: I took your bracelet a while ago. Sorry. Force of habit.
  • Lux: Aw, is that why you're on the run? You keep stealing stuff?
  • Lux: It's okay! I know you've got a good heart. So shine with me!
  • Choncc: Lux fires off a light that catches several training dummies, stopping them in their tracks.
Part 5
  • Choncc: Nimblefoot returns Lux's bracelet. As they do, a letter materializes alongside it: an invite to the Tournament of Souls!
  • Choncc: Excited, Nimblefoot ricochets between the training dummies. Lux follows up with a burst of soul power!
  • Nimblefoot: On the run or right at home...
  • Lux: …everywhere we go, the light follows!

Samira / Squink

Miracles? Who needs them? We have guns. LOTS of guns.

Part 1
  • Choncc: The flames from Samira's flair have lightly scorched Chonne's Dome. Samira aims her weapon at a wanted poster of Viego.
  • Samira: No bounty escapes my eye.
  • Squink: Focus, Samira! Give it your all!
  • Samira: Heh, say less.
Part 2
  • Choncc: Wanted posters from Samira's previous targets lie scattered on the floor. Outlaw Squink stands next to Samira, comparing their guns to her soul weapons.
  • Squink: So... two guns and a sword? I guess that's how you took down all of those bounties!
  • Samira: Sometimes, one just isn't enough to get the job done with style.
  • Squink: It can get a little messy. But it's effective.
Part 3
  • Squink: Hey, Samira. What do you do when a target's too strong to beat?
  • Choncc: Samira dematerializes her weapons and turns away from Squink. Outlaw Squink lowers their guns in response, visibly concerned
  • Samira: Keep fighting, no matter the odds. I won't be afraid... ever again.
  • Squink: But you're not alone anymore! You have a friend you can rely on. Trust me.
Part 4
  • Squink: Ready... BEGIN!
  • Choncc: Samira shoots at multiple Viego-themed training dummies. A 10-second timer rings.... and one dummy still stands.
  • Samira: Tch, times up?! Must be a mistake.
  • Squink: All part of the process! Next time, we'll get it right together!
Part 5
  • Squink: Time for your final test… Think you can keep up?
  • Choncc: Outlaw Squink and Samira face one another in a cowboy standoff. The pair quickly draw their weapons... to mow down dozens of Viego target dummies together!
  • Choncc: Slowly, the dust settles, and an invitation to the Tournament of Souls materializes before the sharpshooting duo.
  • Samira: You're not half-bad, pipsqueak. Time for our biggest thrill yet, eh?

Sett / Shisa

Showing mercy is hard, but nothing is harder than our fists!

Part 1
  • Choncc: Sett and Shisa stretch side by side in Chonce's Dome, warming up their muscles for the workout to come.
  • Shisa: You ready to feel the burn, Sett?
  • Sett: I was born ready! I was born to run the Tournament of Souls!
  • Shisa: That's right! Step aside, Viego! We're coming for your throne!
Part 2
  • Choncc: As their workout officially begins, Sett and Shisa focus on their biceps. With each rep, their luscious locks begin to glow a shinier shade of gold.
  • Shisa: Remember, Sett, these muscles we're building may be big, but the most important muscle...
  • Sett: Are the lats? Delts? No, wait... triceps!
  • Shisa: …It's your heart, Sett! We must always fight with our hearts!
Part 3
  • Choncc: Shisa spots Sett as he bench presses. Sett struggles with the weight, seemingly discouraged.
  • Shisa: C'mon, Sett, you got this. Just keep pushing yourself!
  • Sett: But what if I'm not strong enough, Shisa? What if when I fight in the tournament... I lose? ON LIVE TV?!
  • Shisa: You're the strongest person I know, Sett. Now, let me hear that battle cry! RrrrrOOOOaaaaAAArrrr!
Part 4
  • Sett: Shisa, I've been thinkin'. I may be the strongest person you know, but the strongest person I know... is my momma.
  • Shisa: My mom is a fighter, too! She's the leader of our pack, and she told me that no matter what I do, I'II always make her proud.
  • Shisa: Now, break this rock! And when you do it, think of making your mom the proudest she's ever been! Think of fighting... for her!
  • Choncc: Shisa tosses a massive boulder Sett's way. The boulder shatters on impact with his pecs, while Sett remains unharmed.
Part 5
  • Choncc: From the rubble of the boulder, Sett picks up a strange scroll—It's an invitation to the Tournament of Souls!
  • Sett: We did it, Shisa! Ya pushed me further than I've ever been pushed before. I guess throwing rocks at people is a legitimate workout routine!
  • Shisa: I always believed in you! But I can't take all the credit. Throwing the big rock at you was my mom's idea. She's a brilliant woman!
  • Choncc: Sett and Shisa continue to throw large boulders at each other, like a game of super-strength dodgeball. Rock on, you two, rock on.
