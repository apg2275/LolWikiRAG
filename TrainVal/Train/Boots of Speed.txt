Similar items Slightly Magical Boots Strategy Boots of Speed can be used as a starter item ( along with Health Potions ) on every champion , especially those who require high mobility. However, since the item does not give defensive or offensive stats , you must take extra care with enemy champions. Many advanced players take boots as a starting item for mid lane to dodge enemy skillshots . Trivia Boots of Speed (alongside with Slightly Magical Boots ) are the only basic items which increases your movement speed. While playing Nami , one can bring up Boots of Speed in the shop by searching for "Hat". Cassiopeia is the only champion that can't buy boots.
