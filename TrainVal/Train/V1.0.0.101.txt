League of Legends v1.0.0.101 Champions Sona, the Maven of the Strings Power Chord (Innate) Innate: After 3 spell casts, Sona's next attack will deal 100 − 440 (based on level) magic damage, and reduce the damage the target deals by 30% for 3 seconds. Additionally, Sona's Auras persist for 3 seconds after deactivating. Hymn of Valor (Q) Active: Sona fires bolts of sound, dealing 70 / 120 / 170 / 220 / 270 (+60% AP) magic damage to the nearest two enemies (prioritizes champions). Aura: Sona plays the Hymn of Valor , increasing nearby allied champions Attack Damage and Ability Power by 8 / 12 / 16 / 20 / 24. Aria of Perseverance (W) Active: Sona sends out healing melodies, healing Sona and the most wounded nearby allied champion by 50 / 75 / 100 / 125 / 150 (+60% AP) . Aura: Sona plays the Aria of Perseverance , increasing nearby allied champions Armor and Magic Resist by 8 / 12 / 16 / 20 / 24. Song of Discord (E) Active: Sona energizes nearby allies, granting them 14 / 18 / 22 / 26 / 30% bonus movement speed for 1. 5 seconds. Aura: Sona plays the Song of Discord , granting nearby allied champions 14 / 18 / 22 / 26 / 30 bonus movement speed . Crescendo (R) Active: Sona plays her ultimate chord, forcing enemy champions in a line in front of her to dance, taking 150 / 250 / 350 (+70% AP) magic damage over 2. 5 seconds. Blitzcrank Power Fist Now resets the auto-attack timer. Fiddlesticks Terrify Now grant assists. Garen Courage Damage reduction reduced to 30% from 35%. Perseverance No longer removed by combat dehancing effects such as Blood Scent . Demacian Justice Fixed various bugs that caused it to not work correctly sometimes. Gragas Explosive Cask Now interacts properly with spell shields like Banshee's Veil . Jax Counter Strike Now has a buff showing the duration of the effect. Karthus Death Defied It now also grants 20% death timer reduction to compensate for the 8 seconds Karthus is active after dying. The code has been remade to fix some bugs and usability issues. Kennen Lightning Rush No longer castable while disabled. Kassadin Nether Blade Mana cost reduced to 25 at all ranks from 30 / 40 / 50 / 60 / 70. Riftwalk It no longer shows cast particles over fog of war. It now leaves a buff showing the duration of the increased cost and damage of subsequent casts. Katarina Shunpo No longer ignores enemy spell shields like Banshee's Veil . Killer Instincts No longer reduces true damage when used with Shunpo . Kog'Maw Caustic Spittle Fixed a bug where it was giving multiplicative attack speed rather than base. Rammus Defensive Ball Curl Now be cancelled. Shaco Jack in the Box Fixed a bug where fearing enemies didn't grant an assist. Singed Fling Now deals damage at the start of the spell from when the target hits the ground. Taric Radiance Now has a brief cooldown when activated with the goal of preventing quick cancellation after initiation. Twitch Ambush The first attack after breaking stealth will now benefit from the bonus attack speed. It will now provide its attack speed bonus if you break stealth by casting a spell. Vladimir Crimson Pact No longer heals Vladimir when his ability power increases. Transfusion Fixed a bug where it was not restoring enough health from Spell Vamp. Transfusion's Fixed a bug where its tooltip showed it restoring less health than it did. Zilean Chronoshift Duration reduced to 13 seconds from 15. Unique passive conversion percent dropped to 1. 5 % from 2%. Unique passive changed to: Each time you attack, you gain one maximum mana (3 second cooldown). Disconnected players will now run home if their Recall is interrupted. Disconnected players now gain 1000 Magic Resist after 1 minute of continuous disconnection.
