Strategy Entropy was designed for a front-line fighter to stick to his target on the smaller maps, where short-range dashes and blinks are very strong. The active effect deals damage over time, which can secure a kill on an enemy with an escape. Frozen Mallet was an alternate Legendary item which performs a similar function. It has less damage but a strong slow and more health, making it better on melee fighter champions who need more health to survive being in close-range and a consistent slow to lock down an enemy. Old icons 1st version Trivia Entropy's active ability was intended to replicate the effects of Crest of Cinders , which was not available in Dominion . Veigar's innate ability was originally called "Entropy" until it was replaced to Equilibrium . Until season three, Entropy was the only item that gave the ability to deal true damage . It remains one of three items, along with Skirmisher's Sabre and Stalker's Blade .
