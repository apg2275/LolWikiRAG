Game [ ] In-game shop The build tree has been upgraded to put some respect on the final item (it's larger) as well as accommodate taller trees. Mythic items with an active now have their own special Mythic active border to differentiate them from the normal active items. Mythic item borders now show up in the scoreboard. The builds-into dropdown gained some smarts and can resize based on its content. Emberknife and Hailblade now appear in the starting items section of the support tab if you load in with Smite. Shopkeeper Open Shop voice lines are once again available for champions and skins that have them.
