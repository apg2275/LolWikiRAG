The Perfect Hex Core has one of the highest single-value ability power bonus in the game, at 180 AP at level 18. It is outclassed only by a Seraph's Embrace with 4334 or more mana or a ​ Spellbinder with an active with more than 75 charges. It also combos well with Rabadon's Deathcap , and the two together provide 420 AP (Including Rabadon's passive). While unstated, Viktor does not gain the Glorious Evolution until after he has augmented all of his basic abilities. Similar previous item was Augment Death .
