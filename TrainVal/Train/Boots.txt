Strategy Boots can be used as a starter item ( along with Health Potions ) on every champion , especially those who require high mobility. However, since the item does not give defensive or offensive stats , you must be careful with enemy champions who are stronger than you in this regard due to this purchase. Boots and its upgrades are a particularly cheap source of bonus movement speed, and are thus key to more easily dodge skillshots and for keeping up with the enemy if your champion has few tools to stick with an opponent. Trivia Boots (alongside with Slightly Magical Boots ) are the only basic items which increases your movement speed. During the era of PVP.net , Nami can bring up Boots in the shop by searching for "Hat". Cassiopeia is the only champion that cannot buy boots.
