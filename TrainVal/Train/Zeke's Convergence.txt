Old icons 1st version 2nd version Trivia Zeke's Convergence is named after a character from the webcomic as a reward for the creator Tim Buckley gaining over 1000 referrals. Zeke's Convergence is the fourth iteration of a supportive aura item. Its ancestral origin is Stark's Fervor which was remade into Zeke's Herald and then replaced by Zeke's Harbinger before being developed into its modern form. The search term 'haroldandkumar' is a direct reference to the film Harold & Kumar .
