Similar items The Bloodthirster Notes Sanguine Blade was released for the Dominion game mode where it replaces The Bloodthirster . It is also available in the Twisted Treeline . Multiple Sanguine Blades do not grant extra stacks for every auto attack, nor do they increase the maximum cap of stacks obtainable. Strategy Because Sanguine Blade requires the holder to remain in combat to maintain stacks, it is often beneficial to attack minions while waiting for an opening to ensure that the weapon will be fully stacked when a team fight breaks out.
