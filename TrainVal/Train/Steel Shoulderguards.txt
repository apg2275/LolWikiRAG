Steel Shoulderguards edit Stats +4 attack damage +50 health +50% base health regeneration + 2 per 10 seconds Passive Unique – Spoils of War : Grants a charge every 35 seconds, up to 3 charges. Basic attacks can consume a charge to execute minions below ( 50% / 30% ) of their maximum health . Killing a minion by any means with a charge grants you and the nearest allied champion kill gold . These effects require an allied champion to be nearby . Receive diminishing gold from excessive minion kills. Unique – Quest : Earn 500 using this item to upgrade to Runesteel Spaulders , gaining the Warding active with 3 wards in stock. Limitations Limited to 1 Support / Jungle item. Strategy Steel Shoulderguards is typically purchased by supports who would prefer to assist their lane partner rather than harrass the opposition. Steel Shoulderguards' execute can give supports the ability to assist laners that lack wave clear or who intend to aggressively push lane.
