League of Legends v0.9.25.24 Champions Nasus, the Curator of the Sands Siphoning Strike : Nasus strikes his foe, dealing damage and increasing the power of his future Siphoning Strikes if he slays his target. Wither : Nasus ages his target, decelerating their movement and attack speeds over 5 seconds. Spirit Fire : Nasus unleashes a delayed spirit flame at a location, dealing damage and reducing the armor of enemies who stand on it. Fury of the Sands (Ultimate): Nasus assumes his true form, the Avatar of Death. While in this form he gains additional Health and Attack Speed, and drains nearby enemies' max health and converts it into bonus damage for the duration of the skill.
