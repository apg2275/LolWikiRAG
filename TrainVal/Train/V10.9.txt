Essence Emporium The Essence Emporium returns from April 30, 2020 to May 13, 2020. We are rolling out improvements to the popular Mystery Champie Icon Boxes. We kept all the things players love, but there are a few minor changes to the experience: To get a full list of icons that can be acquired from each box, please check the item description. When you purchase a Mystery Champie Icon Box, it will now appear in the Hextech Crafting tab (where your skin shards, Prestige Points, and event tokens live). If you receive an icon you already own, you can disenchant it for a full refund of the BE cost. Ranked The Summoner Rift Ranked Split 1 rolls into Split 2 on May 3, 2020 (11:59 PM PDT).
