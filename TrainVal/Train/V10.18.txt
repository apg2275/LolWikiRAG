A player's hover card in the Friends list should now accurately reflect a player's current state (In Queue, Champ Select, Online). Game [ ] Health bars Added the ability to show Champion names instead of Summoner names over the health bar. Tooltips The "Show More Info" level-up grid view is now available on all champions. Many champions now have magic damage/physical damage/true damage highlighted to their own specific color (this is not on all champions yet, but will slowly release over time).
