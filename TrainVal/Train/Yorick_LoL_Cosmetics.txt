In Pentakill: Lost Chapter promotional media, Yorick was shown wearing a hat, however, it was not apart of his in game skin in League of Legends . This has lead to the community voicing their objections against the visual discrepancy. In the skin's feedback thread, Riot DW Platypus noted that a toggle would not be included in the skin's release patch, as toggles cannot be easily implemented within the allotted time on PBE, however, they would end their statement by saying that they will look to see if it would be possible implement in one of the remaining releases in 2021.
