Furor was a great enchantment for ADCs , or basic-attack-based bruisers with reliable gapclosers . Furor was best used when champions had enough attack speed to keep the bonus from decaying. Furor was similar to the Rage passive provided by Phage , Trinity Force and Entropy , and could multiply the flat movement speed bonus. Attacking structures did not activate Furor . Ranged champions making use of Runaan's Hurricane and melee champions making use of Tiamat , Ravenous Hydra , or Titanic Hydra could still proc Furor . Pets ' basic attacks did not proc Furor Azir's Arise! soldiers could still proc Furor when attacking, despite doing damage in a small line damage over time .
