Old icons 1st version 2nd version Trivia This item is a reference to the Shojin Monastery and the Shojin Order of Ionia . Awakened Dragon might have been a reference to Lee Sin since he has the ability to channel the " dragon’s spirit " and he arrived at the Shojin Monastery at a young age. Spear of Shojin was first introduced on the PBE for the patch V8.12 , but was delayed multiple times, until it was released with Nexus Blitz . The Awakened Dragon passive was previously on Essence Reaver before patch V9.3 .
