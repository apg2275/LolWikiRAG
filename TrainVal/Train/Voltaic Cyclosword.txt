This includes but is not limited to normal or special forms of movement, using dashes or blinks , or being displaced to another location. Hitting abilities that apply on-hit effects will also generate 6 stacks. Voltaic Cyclosword's Galvanize increases the generation of Energize stacks by 75% for dashes and blinks as well as while moving in stealth . This means it grants 1. 75 stacks for every 24 units travelled during those effects. Firmament deals proc damage , and thus will not trigger spell effects . Firmament is not blocked by spell shield .
