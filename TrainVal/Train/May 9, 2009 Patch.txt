Cooldown reduced to 14 seconds from 18. Ability power ratio changed to 0. 3 from 0. 2 . Black Shield (E) Changed Black Shield to a magic absorbing shield, that causes the bearer to be immune to all magic effects until the shield is burned through magic damage. Enrage Remade Now drains 6 / 8 / 10 / 12 / 14 health per attack from draining health per second while activated. Kills while this skill is active permanently increase Sion's maximum health by 1 / 1. 5 / 2 / 2. 5 / 3 (no cap). Cardmaster Wild Cards (Q) Remade Wild Cards ability moved to the "Q" key and has a new function: Cardmaster throws three cards in front of him which deal damage to each enemy they pass through. Pick a Card (W) Cardmaster draws cards in a sequential order and gets an added effect on his next attack according to the card he has picked. Blue cards add magic damage and the ability to draw another card. Red cards explode, dealing magic damage to all enemies near the target and slowing their movement for 1 second. Gold cards explode, dealing magic damage to all enemies in an area and stunning them for 1. 25 seconds. Destiny Now reveals stealthed units.
