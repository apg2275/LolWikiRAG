Pentakill Olaf This skin shares the Pentakill theme with: Pentakill Karthus Pentakill Kayle Pentakill Mordekaiser Pentakill Sona Pentakill Yorick The playerbase decided he would become Pentakill's drummer instead of Trundle . Mordekaiser embarked on a metal-fueled journey to recruit Karthus , Yorick , Sona , and Olaf in order to form Pentakill so they would Smite and Ignite the whole of Runeterra with their music. [1] He references Vinnie Paul , has angry abs, and his drumsticks resemble the Terror Mask from Splatterhouse . Undertow produces a drum beat upon hitting an enemy and Reckless Swing briefly flashes the old Pentakill band logo.
