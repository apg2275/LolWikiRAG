When detected, these messages will not be sent to other players, the offending player will be system muted, and all players will be notified. With growing confidence in feeding detection, increased strictness in the model for high-level play in Ranked Solo/Duo. Recently honored players will now be visible in the Add Friend and Friend Requests menus. Champion Select Bug Fix: Searching for a champion by typing their name in the search bar will automatically clear any currently selected role filters. Mythic Content Mythic Chroma icons have been updated and standardized to have: a portrait with new colors that reflect changes to the skin, new summoner icon if the original skin did not have a matching icon OR did not have an icon that features their portrait, no decorative border, and naming standardization - Skin Name Mythic Chroma Icon. The following icons receiving changes in patch 12.20 include: Ashen Conqueror Pantheon Mythic Chroma icon, Ashen Knight Pyke Mythic Chroma icon, Battle Academia Ezreal Mythic Chroma icon, Final Boss Veigar Mythic Chroma icon, and Star Guardian Jinx Mythic Chroma icon.
