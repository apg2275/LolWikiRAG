Similar items Ravenous Hydra Titanic Hydra Edge of Night Axiom Arc Hubris Voltaic Cyclosword Youmuu's Ghostblade Umbral Glaive The Collector Serpent's Fang Opportunity Serylda's Grudge Notes Cleave deals default damage , and thus will not trigger spell effects . Heretical Cleave deals area damage , and will trigger spell effects in contrast to Cleave . Cleave is not blocked by spell shield . Cleave does not trigger on attacks against structures. Cleave will not trigger if the basic attack does not deal damage, such as if the target was invulnerable . (bug) This is unlike Tiamat , Ravenous Hydra , and Titanic Hydra , which will trigger Cleave even if the basic attack had dealt no damage. Heretical Cleave is an auto-targeted effect. Heretical Cleave's cast breaks stealth . Heretical Cleave has a cast time equal to the user's attack windup . Heretical Cleave's radius is centered offset from the user in the direction they are facing. Trivia Along with Ravenous Hydra and Titanic Hydra , Profane Hydra borrows its namesake from the Hydra , a many-headed beast.
