Similar items Death's Dance Immortal Shieldbow Maw of Malmortius Sterak's Gage Notes Upon triggering Rebirth , all summoner spells that are not already on cooldown are placed on a 4-second cooldown . Rebirth takes priority over Anivia's Rebirth , Zac's Cell Division , and all zombie state effects but not Zilean's Chronoshift . Rebirth does not activate when killed by the Nexus Obelisk . Rebirth's health restoration is considered healing and is increased by effects such as Spirit Visage (+25% to healing ), which causes it to restore 62. 5 % of base health in total. While Rebirth is in effect, the user's health regeneration is set to 0. Quicksilver can be used during Rebirth . Rebirth's untargetability does not destroy in-flight projectiles .
