Old icons 1st version 2nd version Trivia Dead Man's Plate was first introduced during the Black Market Brawlers Featured Game Mode , and has later been added to all maps after the Bilgewater event, Burning Tides, had ended. Gangplank's pauldron is the Dead Man's Plate , worn by him after his supposed "death" at the hands of Miss Fortune in Act 3 of Burning Tides . The trailing visual effect for maximum Momentum is reused from Sion's Unstoppable Onslaught , but shrunken down slightly. Pentakill's second volume, Pentakill II: Grasp of the Undying , named its fifth track after this item.
