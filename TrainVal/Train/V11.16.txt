Garena Anniversary Master's Challenge Season 8 League of Legends V11.16 [ ] Client [ ] Rotating Game Mode: One for All returns from August 12th, 2021 until September 7th, 2021. Clash The second weekend of the Bandle City-themed Clash will be August 21 and 22. Team formation for the second weekend will be open starting on August 16. Ranked A third penalty tier for queue-dodging has been added. Players that dodge three times in a 24-hour period will experience a 12-hour MOBA queue lockout and a 10 LP loss (the first two dodge penalties are unchanged). Added a new social leaderboard inside Ranked Summoner's Rift lobbies. You can access this leaderboard by clicking on the new leaderboard button in the upper right corner of the Solo/Duo or Flex lobby. Master and above Flex players can no longer queue with other Flex players rated below Platinum. Your Shop Your Shop returns from July 22, 2021 to August 30, 2021 (6:00 PM CEST).
