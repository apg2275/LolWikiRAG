Surprise Party Fiddlesticks League of Legends v1.0.0.127 [ ] Champions [ ] Graves, the Outlaw True Grit (Innate): Graves gains increasing armor and magic resistance the longer he remains in combat. Buckshot : Graves fires three bullets in a cone damaging all enemies in the projectiles path. Enemies at close range can be hit by multiple projectiles, but each one beyond the first will deal reduced damage. Smoke Screen : Graves fires a smoke canister at the target area that deals damage and creates a cloud of smoke. Enemies inside the smoke cloud have reduced sight range and movement speed. Quickdraw : Graves dashes forward gaining an attack speed boost for several seconds. Attacking enemies lowers the cooldown of this skill. Collateral Damage (Ultimate): Graves fires an explosive shell dealing heavy damage to the first target it hits. After hitting a champion or reaching the end of its range the shell explodes dealing damage in a cone behind the target. Corki Gatling Gun Added a base damage of 20 / 32 / 44 / 56 / 68 per second. Dominion [ ] Ambient experience gain has been lowered by 10%. Lowered the point where Nexus Health will only be affected by holding capture points (and not by Champion Kills or Neutralizes) to 100 Nexus Health from 125 Nexus Health. Health packs pickups will now grant 20% more Mana. Personal Scoring has been adjusted to more evenly reward defensive play: Minion Kill reward increased to 2 points from 1. Channel Interrupt reward increased to 5 from 2. Martyr (dying on point) now gives an additional 10 points over its previous bonus. Point defense bonus is granted more regularly. There is now a score bonus associated with getting a champion kill or assist while being on or near a point. Items [ ] Archangel's Staff Again displays current bonus mana. Hextech Gunblade Attack damage reduced to 40 from 60. Ability power reduced to 70 from 75. Life steal reduced to 15% from 20%. Spell vamp reduced to 20% from 25% and is now Unique. Manamune Again displays current bonus mana. Tear of the Goddess Again displays current bonus mana. Hotfix [ ] Haunting Nocturne has received two particle changes on his skills. Duskbringer now has a slime green/light blue particle with green ghosts coming out of it from black. Unspeakable Horror has had its color changed to light blue from black.
