Face of the Mountain edit Stats +10% cooldown reduction +450 health +100% base health regeneration + 2 per 10 seconds Active Unique – Deadly Phalanx : Shield yourself and an ally equal to 10% of your maximum health for 4 seconds. Afterwards, the shield is consumed to slow nearby enemies by 40% for 2 seconds (60 second cooldown). Passive Unique – Spoils of War : Melee basic attacks execute minions below 340 − 680 (based on level) health . Attacks that trigger Spoils of War gain 150 bonus range and slightly increased attack speed . Killing a minion by any means heals you and the nearest allied champion for 50 (+ 2% of missing health) and grants them the kill gold . These effects require an allied champion within 1050 units of the user (20 seconds recharge time; 4 charges). Unique – Bandit : Grants Bandit . Unique – Quest : Earn 750 using this item. Reward: Shield Battery , a permanent shield for 255 − 340 (based on level) . The shield regenerates slowly while out-of-combat. Executing minions with Spoils of War restores shield strength equal to 64 − 85 (based on level) . Limitations Limited to 1 Face of the Mountain . Limited to 1 Gold Income item. Similar items Eye of the Equinox Frost Queen's Claim Locket of the Iron Solari Talisman of Ascension Notes The quest reward can only be obtained after being out-of-combat for 5 seconds. Triggering the Spoils of War passive adds +1 to the creep score of the allied champion who receives the bonus gold. The execute health threshold is affected by the bonus damage from critical strikes and other autoattack modifiers (like Gangplank's Parrrley ), but not from Sheen . The Spoils of War passive is triggered no matter what method is used to kill the minion , though the execute can only be triggered with a melee basic attack. The Deadly Phalanx active can be self-cast. The shield changes colors (from blue to yellow) right before it explodes. As an enemy, you can use this visual indication to avoid the explosion. You can save the four charges and kill four or more minions, and heal nearby allied champions for around (50+20) per charge. This mass heal can help tremendously in a teamfight. On Summoner's Rift , assuming Face of the Mountain 's user kills the most valuable minions available, it provides: 13. 125 per 10 seconds to the user, while also sharing that same gold with the lane partner. Between 20 and 35 minutes, this is increased to 15. 83 per 10 . After 35 minutes, this is further increased to 22. 5 per 10 . Assuming Face of the Mountain's user kills one minion every 20 seconds, it provides at least 12. 5 health regen per 5 seconds to the user and to the lane partner, not taking into account the missing health ratio. Old icons 1st version Trivia Face of the Mountain , Frost Queen's Claim , and Talisman of Ascension are a set of items available since Season Four that were designed for Support . They were themed after locations from Runeterra that all incidentally involve a form of physical transcendence.
