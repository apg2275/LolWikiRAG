His appearance was inspired by Bayek from the Assassin's Creed series. The color scheme of his Sapphire chroma shares a striking resemblance to Prince from Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time and his weapon Dagger of Time . This skin is a nod to one of the possible thematics Riot explored with Pyke, a sand wraith from Shurima.
