Similar items Rectrix Tiamat Caulfield's Warhammer Tunneler Serrated Dirk Phage The Brutalizer Notes Each basic attack generates 6 Energize stacks while 1 is generated for every 24 in-game units traveled. This includes but is not limited to simple movement, using dashes or blinks , or being displaced to another location. Jolt deals proc damage , and thus will not trigger spell effects . Jolt is not blocked by spell shield . Kircheis Shard is an Energized effect and will stack other sources of it. Energized effects will apply their on-hit damage to structures .
