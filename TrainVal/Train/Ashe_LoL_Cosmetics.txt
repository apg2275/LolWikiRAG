to be ambidextrous . The hand she holds her bow with alternates from her left hand and her right hand in the artwork of her skins. Examples of her holding it with her left hand are the art for Classic , Freljord and Sherwood Forest . Examples of her holding it with her right hand are the art for Queen , Amethyst , and Heartseeker . A red recolor of her old Chinese artwork used to be the background image of the Offense branch of the now-retired summoner mastery trees in Season 2 , Season 3 , Season 4 and Season 5 (Season 5 retained Season 4's masteries). Recolors of Soraka's and Poppy's were used for the Utility and Defense branches respectively. Her very first splash art was directly tweaked for the first splash art of her Freljord skin.
