Demon Vi This skin shares the Demonic theme with: Gatekeeper Galio Little Demon Tristana Little Devil Fizz Little Devil Teemo She was inspired by Hellboy . Her right leg reads VI VI VI , referencing "666" Number of the Beast (Roman notation: DCLXVI ). The value is based that of נרון קסר nrwn qsr in Hebrew notation, to cryptically demonize Nero Caesar , known for his extravagance & brutality. She displays the number six (whether Roman or Arabic ) six times (once below her left eye, once on her left leg, three times on her right leg, once on her back). She is one of the servants of Little Devil Teemo .
