Busy Bee League of Legends V11.5 [ ] Client [ ] Rotating Game Mode: ARURF returns to live servers from February 4th, 2021 to March 8th, 2021. League Client Bug Fix: Discovered and fixed an issue that would cause players to be stuck at the "Waiting for Stats" screen instead of directing the player to their end-of-game lobby. Bug Fix: Fixed various rendering issues around the Match History graphs and statistics. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug that sometimes prevented players from trading champions in Champ Select. Game [ ] In-game shop Recommended items now recommend starter bundles, item combinations that are most commonly purchased first based on data. The font rendering has been improved in the item and search detail views. Additional bug fixes and performance improvements. Shurelya's Battlesong now properly appears in the item shop when filtering for "Ability Power" or "Mana & Regeneration". Systems Bug Fix: Champions that are untargetable when a Dragon is slain now properly receive their share of experience from the kill. Champions [ ] Azir Arise! This was done by making Ezreal's current facing direction turn towards Mystic Shot's victim if the hit triggered Runaan's Hurricane's Wind's Fury. Urgot Purge Bug Fix: Each attack beyond the first now properly triggers Muramana's Shock and Ravenous Hydra's Cleave, instead of only triggering on the first attack. Viego Sovereign's Domination New Effect: Enemy champions possessed who own Dark Seal or Mejai's Soulstealer and have Glory stacks will now also be gained by him during the possession. Bug Fix: No longer loses stacks of Glory from Dark Seal and Mejai's Soulstealer upon possessing an enemy champion. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where he could sometimes possess a Mist Wraith from a massive range if cast right at the start of another possession. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where he would sometimes instantly possess Mist Wraiths without the wind up time on them. Removed: No longer pops in-flight turret projectiles when he becomes untargetable from the wind up on a Mist Wraith. Viktor Glorious Evolution Bug Fix: Relic Shield's Spoils of War kills now properly count towards Glorious Evolution . Vladimir General Undocumented/Bug Fix: Blood Orb particle restored. Items [ ] Black Cleaver Total cost reduced to 3100 from 3300 . Combine cost reduced to 850 from 1050 . Health increased to 400 from 300. New Unique Passive - Rage: Dealing physical damage to a champion grants 5 bonus movement speed per stack of Carve on them for 2 seconds, up to 30 . Removed Unique Passive - Butcher: Basic attacks on-hit and abilities that deal physical damage against fully Shredded enemies deal bonus physical damage equal to 5% of their missing health . This is reduced to 2% of their missing health for damage over time effects, which may only deal the bonus damage every 0. 5 seconds. Cull Bug Fix: Relic Shield's Spoils of War kills now properly count towards Cull stacks. Doran's Shield Bug Fix: Ranged champions now properly benefit from the Endure passive (upon taking single target damage). Undocumented/Bug Fix: Melee and Ranged champions now properly benefit from the Endure passive (upon taking area damage or damage over time). Guinsoo's Rageblade New Effect: On-hit damage is now capped at 100% critical strike chance. New Effect: On-hit damage is now affected by critical strike damage modifiers. Maw of Malmortius Total cost reduced to 2800 from 3100 . Combine cost reduced to 400 from 700 . Muramana Unique Passive - Shock: New Effect: Basic attacks against champions deal 1. 5 % maximum mana bonus physical damage on-hit. Damaging abilities against champions deal ( 3. 5 % / 2. 7 % ) maximum mana (+ 6% AD) bonus physical damage . Can only be triggered once from the same attack or cast. On-hit attacks that trigger spell effects count as abilities for this purpose. Old Effect: Basic attacks on-hit and damaging abilities against champions deal 2. 5 % of maximum mana bonus physical damage . Can only be triggered on a single target and once every 4 seconds from the same attack or cast. Bug Fix: Shock will now trigger once per ability per enemy champion , instead of once per ability against the first enemy champion stuck. Undocumented/Bug Fix: The cooldown of this is 6. 5 seconds for damage from the same ability, previously this resetting delay was inconsistent. Rageknife New Effect: On-hit damage is now affected by critical strike damage modifiers. New Effect: On-hit damage is now capped at 100% critical strike chance. Ravenous Hydra New Effect: Cleave now only triggers once every 10 seconds within the same cast instance . Removed: Cleave no longer triggers from spell damage by items. Bug Fix: (Regarding the above) Cleave now behaves more consistently across similar abilities. Relic Shield Bug Fix: Spoils of War kills now properly count towards Viktor's Glorious Evolution and Cull stacks. Runaan's Hurricane New Effect/Bug Fix: Attacks against secondary targets can now trigger Titanic Hydra's effect. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where the bolts would generate additional stacks of Conqueror . Serpent's Fang Unique Passive - Shield Reaver: New Effect: Dealing damage to an enemy champion reduces any shields they gain within the next 3 seconds by ( 50% / 25% ) , and if the target was not already afflicted by this effect, reduces all active shields on them by the same amount. This does not affect magic damage shields. Old Effect: Basic attacks and abilities deal 70 (+ 40% bonus AD) bonus physical damage against shields . Does not affect magic damage shields. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where Shield Reaver would not reduce the target's current shields if the user owned an item with an on-hit effect. Shurelya's Battlesong Bug Fix: Now properly appears in the item shop when filtering for "Ability Power" or "Mana & Regeneration". Stridebreaker Halting Slash slow reduced to 40% from 60%. Halting Slash AD ratio reduced to 75% AD from 100% AD . Halting Slash dash range increased to 300 units from 200. Halting Slash dash speed increased to 300 + 200% movement speed from 300 + 150% movement speed . Undocumented/Bug Fix: Halting Slash can no longer dash through player-generated terrain . Hotfixes [ ] March 3rd Hotfix [ ] Pyke Gift of the Drowned Ones Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where untargetable enemies would not count towards the additional health regen he receives when he's near two or more enemies. Serpent's Fang Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where Shield Reaver passive would not be applied by ability damage. March 4th Hotfix [ ] Azir Emperor's Divide Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where the ability would fail to hit the champions behind him, even if they are visually in the ability's range Back missiles restored. Viego Sovereign's Domination Bug Fix: Dark Seal's stacks no longer underflow if Viego died with the item under certain conditions while transformed, which lead to Viego gaining extreme values of AP . Wukong Stone Skin Bug Fix: Once again grants Wukong the base bonus armor and health regeneration. March 8th Hotfix [ ] Ranked Riot created pro player accounts created after January 7th, 2021, will be disabled due to unintended differences in LP gains and loss which resulted in an unfair advantage over non-pros in reaching the top of the ranked ladder. These accounts will decay out of the ladder over the next few week and pros will be given new accounts with fixed MMR. Graves Smoke Screen Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where it would block abilities when cast on top of Graves . Jayce Acceleration Gate Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where he would gain 2 Muramanas if he upgraded Manamune while casting Acceleration Gate on an ally. Kled Skaarl the Cowardly Lizard Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where Skaarl's total health wasn't being treated as bonus health. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where remounting would be affected Grievous Wounds . Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where Kled's dismounted health would not regenerate past the amount he was at when he remounted. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where Skaarl would not respawn at full health after Kled's been killed. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where would still gain courage while being revived by Guardian Angel .
