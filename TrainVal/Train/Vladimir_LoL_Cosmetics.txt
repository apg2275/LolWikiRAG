Count Vladimir This skin shares the Storybook theme with: Annie in Wonderland Big Bad Warwick Caterpillar Kog'Maw Mad Hatter Shaco Red Riding Annie He was inspired by Vlad the Impaler . He references the titular Count from Dracula by Bram Stoker . Blood Lord Vladimir's chest plate can be seen in the background of his splash art. He resembles Alucard from the Castlevania series. Blood Lord Vladimir This skin shares the Bloodstone theme with: Blood Knight Hecarim Bloodfury Renekton Bloodstone Lissandra Bloodstone Taric Dark Crystal Ryze Nightraven Fiora He is voiced by Gavin Hammon . Pantheon and Varus are also voiced by the same voice actor. He is the second non-comical Legendary skin (the first being Demonblade Tryndamere ). His dance references contact juggling , specifically based on the performance done in this video. A side-by-side comparison can be seen here. Soulstealer Vladimir This skin shares the Soulstealer theme with: Soulstealer Vayne He is depicted as if he were from the Shadow Isles and carries a soul container resembling Thresh's lantern . Academy Vladimir This skin shares the Academy theme with: Academy Ahri Academy Darius Academy Ekko Headmistress Fiora Professor Ryze The group is in classroom 1337, which references Leetspeak .
