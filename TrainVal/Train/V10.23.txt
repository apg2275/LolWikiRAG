Statistics Ability Haste / Cooldown Reduction Cooldown reduction has been replaced by a new stat called Ability Haste . Ability Haste's calculation: Base Cooldown ÷ (1 + AH ÷ 100). Cooldown reduction's calculation: Base Cooldown × (1 − CDR ÷ 100). Summoner Spell Cooldown Reduction has been replaced by a new stat called Summoner Spell Haste which reduces summoner spell cooldowns by the same calculation. Active Item Cooldown Reduction has been replaced by a new stat called Item Haste which reduces the cooldowns for item actives and passives by the same calculation. Ability Haste's power stacks linearly and is uncapped. Removed: Cooldown reduction's 40% hard cap, 45% with Cosmic Insight . Examples: 10% CDR = +11. 11 % cast efficiency ( × 0. 9 effective cooldown, 10% reduction = 11. 11 AH) 40% CDR = +66. 67 % cast efficiency ( × 0. 6 effective cooldown, 40% reduction = 66. 67 AH) 10 AH = +10% cast efficiency ( × 0. 91 effective cooldown, 9. 09 % reduction = 9. 09 % CDR) 40 AH = +40% cast efficiency ( × 0. 71 effective cooldown, 28. 57 % reduction = 28. 57 % CDR) 67 AH = +67% cast efficiency ( × 0. 6 effective cooldown, 40. 12 % reduction = 40. 12 % CDR) Life steal New Effect: Certain on-hit effects from items now benefit from life steal. The following items benefit from life steal: Ardent Censer , Black Cleaver , Blade of the Ruined King , Divine Sunderer , Essence Reaver , Guinsoo's Rageblade , Lich Bane , Muramana , Nashor's Tooth , Rageknife , Recurve Bow , Sheen , Titanic Hydra , Trinity Force , Wit's End , Zeke's Convergence . The following items do not benefit from life steal: Dead Man's Plate , Doran's Ring , Doran's Shield , Duskblade of Draktharr , Kircheis Shard , Kraken Slayer , Noonquiver , Rapid Firecannon , Stormrazor , Tear of the Goddess . Note: Sheen and its upgrades already applied life steal beforehand. Blade of the Ruined King also did, but indirectly. Spell vamp (engine-level drain from spell damage types) stat is no longer available from any items nor runes. New Vamp stat that divides into two subcategories: Physical vamp and Omnivamp . Each Vamp type heals a portion of the damage you deal, reduced to 33% effectiveness for AoE and pet damage. Physical vamp applies to all physical damage dealt. Omnivamp applies to all damage dealt, including magic damage , physical damage , and true damage .
