Seraphine - New champion Stage Presence (Innate) Innate - Echo: Seraphine basic abilities generate a stack of Echo , stacking up to 2 times. At 2 stacks, Seraphine's next basic ability casts an additional time at no cost after a 0. 033 seconds delay, consuming all Echo stacks after the cast time of the second cast completes. Seraphine gains full stacks of Echo when the game starts and upon respawning. Innate - Harmony: Seraphine's abilities grant a Note to her and all nearby allied champions, lasting 6 seconds, up-to 4 Notes at a time can be present per unit. Seraphine's next basic attack gains 25 bonus attack range per active Note and fires all of them at the target, dealing 5 − 20 (based on level) (+ 7. 5 % AP) magic damage for each Note discharged. Subsequent Notes deal 95% of the previous Note's damage to non-minions. Notes deal 200% bonus damage to minions. Effect Radius: 800 . Speed: 3000 . High Note (Q) Active: Seraphine hurls a soundwave to the target location that quickly expands in a radius upon impact, dealing 55 / 65 / 75 / 85 / 95 (+ 55% AP) magic damage to enemies in the area, increased by 0% − 50% (based on target's missing health) . Mana Cost: 65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85. Cooldown: 10 / 8. 75 / 7. 5 / 6. 25 / 5 seconds. Target Range: 900. Effect Radius: 350. Speed: 1200. Cast Time: 0. 25 seconds. Surround Sound (W) Active: Seraphine inspires nearby ally champions with her music, granting herself 20% (+ 4% per 100 AP) decaying bonus movement speed and her allies 8% (+ 1. 6 % per 100 AP) bonus movement speed . She and her allies are also shielded for 60 − 120 (based on level) (+ 30% AP) for 2. 5 seconds. Surround Sound will heal Seraphine and all nearby allied champions for 5% (+ 1. 125 % per 100 AP) of missing health per ally after a 2. 5 -second delay if Seraphine already had a shield at the time of cast. Surround Sound's effects can stack up to 2 times. Mana Cost: 50 / 70 / 90 / 110 / 130. Cooldown: 26 / 25 / 24 / 23 / 22 seconds. Effect Radius: 800 . Cast Time: 0. 25 seconds. Beat Drop (E) Active: Seraphine fires a heavy soundwave in a line in the target direction, dealing 60 / 85 / 110 / 135 / 160 (+ 35% AP) magic damage to enemies struck, reduced to 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100% against minions, and slowing them by 99% for 1 second. Enemies that are already slowed are also rooted for the same duration. Enemies that are immobilized or grounded are also stunned for the same duration. Mana Cost: 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100. Cooldown: 13 / 12 / 11 / 10 / 9 seconds. Target Range: 1300. Width: 140. Speed: 1200. Cast Time: 0. 25 seconds. Encore (R) Active: Seraphine projects a captivating force in the target direction that deals 150 / 200 / 250 (+ 60% AP) magic damage to all enemies struck and charms , slows , and reveals them for 1. 25 / 1. 5 / 1. 75 seconds. Encore's projectile resets its remaining travel distance whenever it strikes an allied or enemy champion, excluding Seraphine . Additionally, all struck allied champions gain 4 Notes . Mana Cost: 100. Cooldown: 160 / 140 / 120 seconds. Target Range: 1200. Width: 320. Speed: 1600. Cast Time: 0. 5 seconds.
