Notes Quest's execute against minions counts as a minion kill for the allied champion that was granted the kill gold. "Receives diminishing gold from excessive minion kills" : Before 5 minutes, killing 7 minions or more reduces gold from minion kills by 80%. After 5 minutes, killing 4 minions per minute causes the gold from minion kills to be reduced by 50% − 80% (based on number of minion kills) . This penalty accounts for all champions on a team that have a Support item; every champion with the item will contribute towards hitting the gold penalty.
