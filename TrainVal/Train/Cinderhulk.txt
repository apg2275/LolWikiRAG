Immolate's damage is increased by 300% against minions and monsters . Similar items Bloodrazor Runic Echoes Sunfire Cape Warrior Notes Aura VFX Fire Nova VFX The aura and nova radii increase if their owner increases in size , but are not impacted by their base size. They cannot get reduced below their base radii by negative size modifiers. Cinderhulk is inactive while out-of-combat. Immolate applies spell effects as default damage . The Immolate aura does not do lethal damage to large jungle monsters with buffs (i.e. Blue Sentinel and Red Brambleback ). This is so that the burn does not accidentally steal a buff that's being donated. Health gained through Sion's Soul Furnace or Cho'Gath's Feast will be increased by the passive. However, there can be a delay in the tool tip updating, so it will not appear straight away sometimes.
