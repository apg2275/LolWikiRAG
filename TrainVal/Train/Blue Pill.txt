Recipe Cost Sell ID 600 240 N/A Availability Menu Tools>Consumable Blue Pill was a consumable item from the alpha stage of League of Legends . Notes The items Blue Pill , Ancient Pocket Watch , Blink Dagger , Scroll of Teleportation , and Yordle Stompers predated and were reworked into summoner spells . Blue Pill was reworked into Recall , a summoner spell; and then eventually reworked again into Recall ; a feature inherent in all champions. Trivia Blue Pill is possibly a reference to the film The Matrix . In the film, Neo is offered the chance to take a red or a blue pill, signifying his choice of whether to continue living in the world he knows or to wake up.
