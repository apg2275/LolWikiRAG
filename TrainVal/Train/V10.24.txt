League Client Updates Improvements to the Champion Select grid, providing a smoother and more reliable experience. It'll take less time to ban and lock in champions, and animations will feel smoother. The Reconnect button should no longer display after a match has completed. Players should now experience the appropriate end-of-game process. The player chat has been updated to the new UI tech we’re using in the shop. Bug Fix: When the minimum resolution for the client is selected, Summoner Spell descriptions should not longer be blocked. Bug Fix: Skin chromas no longer show the incorrect champion. Bug Fix: The "This skin is currently not available to purchase" tooltip should no longer appear when entering Champion Select. Leaver Buster If there is an AFK player detected on your team after the 10-minute mark, a surrender vote can occur. For now, this change will only apply to Summoner's Rift and the vote must be unanimous. If the vote isn’t unanimous, at the 15-minute mark the team can vote again, now only needing a majority vote. Ranked If a player on your team leaves during a ranked match or is detected as AFK, your LP loss may be reduced. Premades with a disconnect/AFK, provisional players, and players in promotion series will not receive this loss reduction. Game [ ] Item Shop The font size of the shop’s gold display has been embiggened. Recommended item card titles are now bolder . When there is only one or two actual recommendations, no empty item cards will appear in the shop. Player chat will now display behind the item shop until you decide to open it. Selected items in the shop will now have some magic surrounding them. Darkened the borders of items you can’t afford to better differentiate them from the ones you can. Made it easier to determine when a recommended item is not affordable. While the shop is opened, you can now open the extended stats panel. Bug Fix: Multiple fixes to items appearing or not appearing in the correct tabs and filter groups. Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where the tags in recommended item cards had too much padding.
