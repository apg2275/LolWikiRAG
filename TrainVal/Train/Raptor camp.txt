Edit The Crimson Raptor and Raptor are neutral monsters on Summoner's Rift . The Raptor camp consists of one large Crimson Raptor and five lesser Raptors . Killing this camp awards gold and experience . Notes [ ] Without slows, the Crimson Raptor has a movement speed of 443 . When its patience runs out its movement speed is increased by 20%, to 500 . Without slows, the Raptor has a movement speed of 492. 5 . When its patience runs out its movement speed is increased by 20%, to 545 . Crimson Raptor deals 3% of the target's current health bonus physical damage with its attacks. Smite cannot be used on small monsters. Trivia [ ] The Raptor camp is a replacement for the Wraith camp on Summoner's Rift since patch V4.20 . Veteran players still sometimes refer to the Raptors as Wraiths . Crimson Raptor replaced the Wraith . Raptor replaced the Lesser Wraiths . A common nickname for the Raptors is chickens . Helmet Bro's peculiar headgear can be seen in-game, inside a nest within the blue side Crimson Raptor's camp. In Teamfight Tactics Sets 1, 2 and 4, the Crimson Raptor and Raptors feature as PvE Round monsters in Round 4–7. The Raptor camp features in the icon of Ivern's Friend of the Forest ability.
