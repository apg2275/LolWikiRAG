Knight's Vow edit Stats +15 ability haste +45 armor +300 health Active Unique – Pledge : Designate the target allied champion as being Worthy , forming a tether between you and them. Champions can only be designated as Worthy by one Knight's Vow at a time. You cannot be designated as Worthy by an ally's Knight's Vow (60 second cooldown; 1250 range). Passive Unique – Sacrifice : While your Worthy ally is tethered to you and you are above 30% of your maximum health , redirect 10% of the pre-mitigation physical and magic damage they take to you as true damage and heal for 10% of the damage dealt by your Worthy ally to champions. If they are below 30% maximum health , the damage redirection is increased to 20%. Limitations Limited to 1 Knight's Vow .
