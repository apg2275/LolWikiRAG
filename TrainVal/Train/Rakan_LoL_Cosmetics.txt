He resembles Tumnus from Narnia . He wears a shoulder pad made from a Raptor's skull and adorned with a couple of Xayah's feathers (she wears an identical one adorned with a couple of his ). He shares his splash art with Xayah . They are the first champions to share their Classic splash art, the other two being Kayle and Morgana .
