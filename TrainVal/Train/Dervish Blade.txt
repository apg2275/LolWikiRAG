Similar items Maw of Malmortius Mercurial Scimitar Wit's End Strategy Dervish Blade worked best as a late game upgrade for AD carries from Quicksilver Sash . Dervish Blade was a great escaping tool, but in order to utilize the movement buff and the removal of crowd control, the active was best combined with blink abilities such as Flash . If Quicksilver Sash was on cooldown and you upgraded it into Dervish Blade , you recovered the ability to use the active again. Dervish Blade removed Grievous Wounds from sources other than Ignite , unlike Cleanse . The active did not cancel or remove Airborne directly, but allowed wielders to use mobility spells and Flash to overrule the effect. Trivia The item's name and named item effect is derived from the chemical element mercury , which is also known as quicksilver. The item's icon is reused for the Teamfight Tactics item Cursed Blade .
