Touch of the Void Edit Description Gain a Touch of the Void stack for each Voidgrub slain, up to a maximum of 6. Your basic attacks against structures are empowered to apply a burn for 4 seconds that deals ( 4 − 24 / 3 − 18 ) (based on stacks) true damage every 0. 5 seconds, for a total of ( 32 − 192 / 24 − 144 ) (based on stacks) . The duration refreshes on subsequent attacks. Gaining 5 or 6 stacks grants the Hunger of the Void buff, which summons allied Voidmites while in combat with structures. These Voidmites will target and attack the nearest enemy structure. Stats Duration Permanent Source Voidgrub Affect Team Touch of the Void is a team-wide stacking neutral buff granted by slaying Voidgrubs . It is available only on Summoner's Rift .
