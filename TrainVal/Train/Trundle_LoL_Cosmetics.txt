Traditional Trundle This skin shares the Traditional theme with: Traditional Karma Traditional Lee Sin Traditional Sejuani This is his first incarnation before he was reworked . He uses his old voiceover together with VFX similar to his old ability kit. In his splash artwork, Original Trundle faces are etched into his chair.
