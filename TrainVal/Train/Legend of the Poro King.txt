This article was last edited by DutyS12345 on 16-Apr-2021 05:57 . Legend of the Poro King mode Legend of the Poro King is a temporary game mode released for the Snowdown Showdown event. Its first appearance was in 2014. Players fight on the Howling Abyss , with the goal is still to destroy the enemy nexus. Beside that, players try to summon the Poro King to aid sieging.
