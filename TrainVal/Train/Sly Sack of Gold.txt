Recipe Cost Sell ID Special 65 2319 Availability Keywords kleptomancy Sly Sack of Gold was a consumable item in League of Legends . Required Kleptomancy . Notes The only gold values that can be obtained by consuming the Sly Sack of Gold are tens (i.e. 40, 50, 60, 70, etc.). There are 8 possible gold values you can obtain. Out of those eight, three of them (40, 50, and 60) are worth less than the selling price (65 gold). This means that, by consuming the item, you have a 62. 5 % chance of getting more gold than you would have by selling the item. Trivia Sly Sack of Gold behaves similarly to Your Cut . The item's icon is reused from the Season 2 and Season 3 mastery Greed 's icon.
