Bio-Arcane Barrage (Q) Kog'Maw's attacks gain range and deal a percent of the target's maximum health as magic damage. Void Ooze (W) Kog'Maw launches a peculiar ooze which damages all enemies it passes through and leaves a trail which slows enemies who stand on it. Caustic Spittle (E) Passive: Increases armor penetration. Active: Kog'Maw launches a corrosive projectile which deals magic damage and doubles his passive armor penetration for 4 seconds (Removes the passive until available again). Living Artillery (Ultimate) Kog'Maw fires a living artillery shell at a great distance dealing damage and revealing targets.
