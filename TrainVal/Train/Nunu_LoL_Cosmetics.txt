Grungy Nunu & Willump This skin shares the Toy Box theme with: Dino Gnar Moo Cow Alistar Pug'Maw Ragdoll Poppy Renektoy Sewn Chaos Orianna Toy Soldier Gangplank Renektoy and toy versions of Tibbers , Original Teemo , Cottontail Teemo and Grungy Willump can be seen in the background. Nunu references Boo while Willump references Sulley (both of which are characters from the animated Disney Pixar film, Monsters, Inc. ). Svellsongur is replaced with a toy recorder . Grungy Nunu Nunu is a reference to Oscar the Grouch while Willump is a reference to the Cookie Monster , both from Sesame Street . Nunu & Willump Bot This skin shares the Heavy Metal theme with: Full Machine Viktor Full Metal Jayce Full Metal Pantheon Full Metal Rammus The background of their splash art is the same arena as in Full Metal Pantheon splash art. "WILLUMP" is an acronym for Workframe Integrated Lossless Lifeform Uploaded Mainly for Punching . Svellsongur is replaced with a wrench secured to Nunu's back. Nunu fires energy blasts from his wrench during Snowball Barrage . Their joke creates a holographic projection of old Nunu Bot . Their dance is likely a reference to the " Disco Nunu " meme, where one would gamethrow by dancing in the middle of a lane in order to get killed by the enemy while using Clairvoyance and Scrying Orb to give the appearance of a disco ball. Nunu Bot He was named after and references the eponymous series by 'angrygoran' . His voice differs from the series because, according to 'angrygoran' himself , another one had to be used due to copyright concerns.
