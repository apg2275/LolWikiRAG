Void Bringer Illaoi This skin shares the Curse of the Void theme with: Pre-Void Kassadin Void Fizz Void Nocturne She is a prophet of The Void just like Malzahar is. Nagakabouros being a Voidborn entity was inspired by Vel'Koz . Coincidentally it might have been the 'Void Kraken' who grabbed Pre-Void Kassadin . The third eye on her forehead references a Voidling . [1] Other Illaoi skin candidates were 'Adventurer Illaoi' and 'Deep Space Illaoi'. [2] She was initially conceived as Battlecast Illaoi during the voting phase, but was renamed towards completion. [3] The skin production process was made transparent with the community through Nexus posts. These included stages such as preproduction, [4] concepting, [5] modeling and texturing, [6] visuals and sound effects, [7] renaming [3] & finalization. [8] She leads the resistance against Creator Viktor and his battlecast machines.
