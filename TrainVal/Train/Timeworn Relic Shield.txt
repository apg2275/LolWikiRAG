Timeworn Relic Shield edit Stats +50 health + 2 per 10 seconds Passive Unique – Spoils of War : Grants a charge every 40 seconds, up to 2 charges. Melee basic attacks can consume one charge to execute minions below 200 − 285 (based on level) (+ 100% AD) health . Killing a minion by any means with a charge heals you and the nearest allied champion for 5 − 30 (based on missing health) , reduced by 50% if the user is ranged , and grants them kill gold . These effects require an allied champion within 1050 units of the user. Unique – Quest : Earn 750 using this item and upgrade to Timeworn Targon's Brace . Reward: Shield Battery , a permanent shield for 255 − 340 (based on level) health. The shield regenerates slowly while out-of-combat. Executing minions with Spoils of War restores shield strength equal to 64 − 85 (based on level) . Limitations Limited to 1 Jungle or Gold Income item. Triggering the Spoils of War passive adds +1 to the creep score of the allied champion who receives the bonus gold. The Spoils of War passive is triggered no matter what method is used to kill the minion , but the execution aspect of the ability only works with melee basic attacks . Assuming Timeworn Relic Shield's user kills the most valuable minions available, Spoils of War provides: 8. 125 per 10 seconds to the user, while also sharing that same gold with the lane partner. Between 20 and 35 minutes, this is increased to 10. 3 per 10 . After 35 minutes, this is further increased to 13. 25 per 10 . Trivia Timeworn Ancient Coin , Timeworn Relic Shield , and Timeworn Spellthief's Edge , are a set of items available since Season Eight that were designed for Support . They were themed after locations from Runeterra that all incidentally involve a form of physical transcendence. This item is a relic of an older iteration of Relic Shield in the recipe of Face of the Mountain , which was changed to allow champions to obtain more diverse build paths. [1]
