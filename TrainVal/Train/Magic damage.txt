For the other types of damage see: Physical damage and True damage . Magic damage is one of the three types of damage in League of Legends . Magic damage is dealt by a portion of all damaging spells from champions , items , runes , and buffs . Statistics influencing magic damage [ ] Magic damage spells commonly scale off of health and/or the offensive statistics attack damage and ability power , but other scalings may also be used. Magic resistance mitigates received magic damage by a percentage. The effectiveness of magic resistance can be reduced by magic penetration .
