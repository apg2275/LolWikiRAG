Bird of Prey Anivia This skin shares the Wonders of the World theme with: Bear Cavalry Sejuani Masked Shaco Oktoberfest Gragas Outback Renekton Panda Teemo ...and others Sultan Tryndamere Uncle Ryze Viking Tryndamere Vizier Malzahar Winged Hussar Xin Zhao She resembles a Bald Eagle . She is holding the Philosopher's Stone in one of her talons. In June 2014, the skin received a unique voiceover in the Hungarian localization; also it was renamed from Ragadozó Anivia to Turulmadár Anivia . Later that month it was announced that the skin was going to represent Hungary and EUNE players with the Hungarian client would get it for free within 3 weeks after the announcement. [1] This caused major controversy in the Hungarian community, as they were expecting a new skin, considering the fact that previous regional skins were created specifically to represent their cultures ( Winged Hussar Xin Zhao – Poland, Sultan Gangplank / Sultan Tryndamere – Türkiye, River Spirit Nami – Brazil, etc.). Moreover, the skin was originally designed with the resemblance to the aforementioned bald eagle, having little in common with the Turul – a national symbol of Hungary.
