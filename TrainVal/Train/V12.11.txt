Bel'Veth - New champion Death in Lavender (Innate) Innate: After casting an ability, Bel'Veth gains 2 stacks for 5 seconds, stacking up to 6 times and refreshing on subsequent ability casts. While Bel'Veth has stacks, she becomes ghosted and gains 25% − 50% (based on level) bonus attack speed . Her basic attacks each consume 1 stack. Innate: Whenever Bel'Veth scores a takedown against a champion or monster within 3 seconds of damaging them, she gains stacks of Lavender permanently. Large monsters grant her 1 stack and champions and epic monsters grant her 2. Each stack grants her 0. 25 % − 1. 25 % (based on level) bonus attack speed . Bel'Veth can exceed the attack speed cap, but all sources of damage dealt or triggered by her basic attacks are reduced by 25%, including on-hit effects. She cannot gain attack speed through growth (per level). Void Surge (Q) Active: Bel'Veth dashes a fixed distance in a designated direction, though not through terrain, dealing 10 / 15 / 20 / 25 / 30 (+ 110% AD) physical damage to enemies she passes through and applying on-hit and spell effects at 75% effectiveness to the first target hit. Void Surge can critically strike for (75% + 40% ) bonus physical damage . Void Surge deals 120% damage to monsters and 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100% damage against minions. Void Surge can only be cast in each of Bel'Veth's cardinal directions with a cooldown that incurs between casts. Each direction has a unique cooldown that is reduced equivalent to 0. 25 ability haste per 1% bonus attack speed . These cooldowns reset upon respawning. Void Surge resets Bel'Veth's basic attack timer. Cooldown: 1 second. Per-Direction Cooldown: 16 / 15 / 14 / 13 / 12 seconds. Range: 400, Collision Radius: 100 , Cast Time: None. Speed: 800 / 850 / 900 / 950 / 1000 + 100% movement speed . Above and Below (W) Active: Bel'Veth slams her tail down in the target direction, dealing 70 / 110 / 150 / 190 / 230 (+ 100% bonus AD) (+ 125% AP) magic damage to enemies struck, knocking them up for 0. 75 seconds, and slowing them by 50% for 1. 25 / 1. 5 / 1. 75 / 2 / 2. 25 seconds. Hitting an enemy champion with Above and Below resets Void Surge's dash cooldown of the direction they were hit. Cooldown: 12 / 11 / 10 / 9 / 8 seconds. Range: 0 - 660, Width: 200, Cast Time: 0. 5 seconds. Royal Maelstrom (E) Active: Bel'Veth rapidly slashes at the nearest enemy with the lowest current health percentage 6 (+ 1 per 33. 3 % bonus attack speed ) times over 1. 5 seconds, dealing 8 / 11 / 14 / 17 / 20 (+ 6% AD) physical damage with each slash, increased by 0% − 300% (based on target's missing health) , and applying on-hit , on-attack , and spell effects at 6% − 24% (based on target's missing health) effectiveness. Royal Maelstrom deals 150% damage to monsters and its attacks apply life steal but cannot critically strike . Bel'Veth gains 70% damage reduction, 20 / 22 / 24 / 26 / 28% (+ 100% life steal ) life steal, and is unable to move during Royal Maelstrom . She cannot perform slashes while unable to declare basic attacks. Royal Maelstrom can be recast after 0. 75 seconds within the duration, and does so automatically afterwards or when Bel'Veth casts an ability. Recast: Bel'Veth ends Royal Maelstrom . Cooldown: 22 / 20 / 18 / 16 / 14 seconds. Effect Radius: 500, Cast Time: None. Endless Banquet (R) Passive: Bel'Veth's basic attacks on-hit apply a mark to the target for 5 seconds, refreshing on subsequent attacks and expiring upon attacking a new target. Every second attack on-hit against the marked target deals 6 / 8 / 10 (+ 12% bonus AD) bonus true damage and grants a stack that increases this damage by the same value. This effect stacks infinitely, but is capped at 5 stacks against epic monsters . Additionally, whenever Bel'Veth scores a takedown against a champion or epic monster while being alive, a Void Coral is spawned from their corpse for 15 seconds. Baron Nashor and Rift Herald summon an enhanced Void Coral . Active: Bel'Veth dashes to the target Void Coral and consumes it over the cast time, slowing nearby enemies by 25%, which ramps up to 99% over the duration, then creates an explosion that deals 150 / 200 / 250 (+ 100% AP) (+ 25 / 30 / 35% of target's missing health) true damage to them. Upon consumption, Bel'Veth also consumes all other existing Void Corals , gains a stack of Lavender per Void Coral consumed, and assumes her True Form for 60 seconds. If Bel'Veth consumed at least one enhanced Void Coral , she gains an enhanced True Form that lasts 180 seconds and causes Void Remora to spawn from the deaths of nearby lane minions . Void Remora have 70% of the minion's maximum health and 110% of the minion's AD . A nearby Void Coral is required to cast this ability. True Form: Bel'Veth evolves into a monster, gaining 100 / 150 / 200 (+ 165% bonus AD) (+ 90% AP) bonus health , 25 / 50 / 75 bonus movement speed out-of-combat, 50 / 75 / 100 bonus attack range, as well as 10 / 15 / 20% increased total attack speed . She also gains the ability to dash through terrain with Void Surge . Consuming Void Corals while in this state refreshes the duration of True Form and heals Bel'Veth for 100 / 150 / 200 (+ 165% bonus AD) (+ 90% AP) . Cooldown: 1 second.
