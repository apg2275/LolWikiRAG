This bonus lasts for 0. 5 seconds upon exiting brush or stealth. Unseen Predator occurs regardless of whether or not his target has sight of him. Ferocity: Rengar generates 1 Ferocity whenever he damages an enemy with an ability . Upon reaching 5 Ferocity , his next basic ability is empowered, can be cast independently of its basic version's cooldown , incurs no cooldown of its own, and consumes all of his Ferocity . Bonetooth Necklace: Rengar collects 1 trophy when scoring a takedown against enemy champions . 3 Trophies : Rengar gains 25 bonus movement speed while out of combat or in brush. 6 Trophies : Unseen Predator gains 125 bonus range. 12 Trophies : Thrill of the Hunt's duration is increased by 5 seconds. 20 Trophies : Thrill of the Hunt's bonus movement speed while stealthed is doubled. Head of Kha'Zix : Rengar gains all combined bonuses from 20 trophies as well as increased sight range while in brush.
