Similar items Executioner's Calling Oblivion Orb Warden's Mail Notes Thorns deals reactive damage , and thus will not trigger spell effects . Thorns is not blocked by spell shield . Damage is reflected when hit by a basic attack , regardless of whether the attack deals damage. If the attacker is blinded , or the wearer is protected by a shield or invulnerability , damage will still be reflected. Healing effects triggering on a basic attack ( life steal , Xin Zhao's Determination , etc.) will first be reduced by Grievous Wounds . This means that the Grievous Wounds , applied by Bramble Vest , will be effective with the basic attack that triggers the effect. Strategy Bramble Vest is a particularly effective defensive option when facing down opponents whose damage is dealt primarily through basic attacks, or against opponents that utilize healing from attacks to survive fights (such as from life steal). Unlike Molten Shield and Defensive Ball Curl , Bramble Vest's magic damage does not trigger spell effects .
