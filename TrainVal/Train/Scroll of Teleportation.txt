Scroll of Teleportation edit Consume Teleports to an allied structure after channeling for 4 seconds. Recipe Cost Sell ID Special No Sell N/A Availability Menu Tools>Consumable Scroll of Teleportation was a starter item from the alpha stage of League of Legends . Notes The items Scroll Of Teleportation , Ancient Pocket Watch , Blink Dagger , Blue Pill , and Yordle Stompers predated and were reworked into summoner spells . Scroll Of Teleportation and Ancient Pocket Watch were reworked into Teleport . Trivia This item bears similarity to an item in Dota 2 that has very similar effects called the Town Portal Scroll .
