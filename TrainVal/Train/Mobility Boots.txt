Map-Specific Differences Arena differences edit This item is disabled. Strategy Mobility Boots is especially useful if you don't have Teleport to switch lanes. Mobility Boots is good in particular early-mid game on roaming champions such as junglers or mid laners, that want to quickly gank different lanes . They lose most value when team fights start to happen. It also allows for chasing and escaping. Mobility Boots is very cheap, making it effective on any champion that is constantly returning to base early game and wants a quick and cheap way to return to lane without Teleport . Being in-combat means engaging in combat with enemies. Affecting allies does not cause you to lose Mobility Boots' bonus. Trivia
