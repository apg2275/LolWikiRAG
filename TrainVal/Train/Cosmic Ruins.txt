Cosmic Ruins Titles Alias (es) Remains of a Broken World Physical characteristics General environment (s) Desolate Universe (s) Event Horizon Cosmic Ruins was a Featured game mode map for the game mode Dark Star: Singularity during the 2017 Dark Star skin event. Contents 1 Lore 2 Features 3 Trivia 4 Media 5 References Lore [ ] The Cosmic Ruins are a corrupted corner of a once-great world that has been harvested and ejected into the vast emptiness of space. A black hole looms in the center of the map, pulling all survivors into the infinite depths of the Dark Star. Features [ ] The map is a heavily modified Summoner's Rift map. All in-game assets updated to be appropriate for Dark Star thematic including: Cosmic background, portals, void hook poles, singularities, purple brush, void Rift Scuttler , etc. The announcer is replaced by Dark Star Thresh . There are no shops or shopkeepers, since all players start with all of their items. Trivia [ ] It is possible that this location is situated between the boarders of regular space and The Void .
