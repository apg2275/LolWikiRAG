Michael 'IronStylus' Maurino once stated the item is now in Sejuani's possession (how the Fury of the North got it is uncertain). [1] Whether this remains canon is unknown. The bonuses from Seeker's Armguard's passive disappear upon upgrading it to Zhonya's Hourglass . However, the stats on Zhonya's Hourglass are already higher than a fully stacked Seeker's Armguard and Fiendish Codex combined so no stats are lost. Seeker's Armguard is a replacement to the Zhonya's Hourglass recipe's former use of a Chain Vest , and a Needlessly Large Rod serving two purposes: the first being to replace the armor value that a Chain Vest would have provided, and the second being to feed in a higher portion of ability power than a Needlessly Large Rod alone would provide.
