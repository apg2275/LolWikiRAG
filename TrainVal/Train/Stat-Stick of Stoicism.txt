Trivia It uses the same icon as The Brutalizer . The item's name references the stat-stick gaming term as well as the stoicism school of philosophy, possibly Daniel 'ZenonTheStoic' Klein who was one of the game designers developing Nexus Blitz. Even though it was unavailable on every map, Stat-Stick of Stoicism could be seen in the item sets on the League Client during patch V8.21 . Stat-Stick of Stoicism started as a debug item, probably used by the game-developing team of Riot Games Inc. [1]
