Spirit of the Elder Lizard edit Stats +30 attack damage +10% cooldown reduction Passive Unique – Incinerate : Dealing physical damage deals 16 − 50 (based on level) bonus true damage over 3 seconds. Unique – Butcher : Against monsters, deal 20% bonus damage and restore 6% of damage dealt to them as health and 3% as mana . Unique – Conservation : Generate Conservation stacks every 1. 5 seconds, up to 80. Killing a large monster consumes up to 40 stacks and grant 1 per stack consumed. Limitations Limited to 1 Spirit of the Elder Lizard . Limited to 1 Gold Income item.
