League of Legends version 1.0.0.61 Champions Udyr The Animal Spirit Monkey's Agility (Innate) Entering a stance grants Udyr a temporary buff that increases his attack speed by 12% and dodge chance by 6%. This effect can stack. Tiger Stance (Q) Active: Udyr's next attack will deal a high amount of damage over 2 seconds (based on his attack damage). Persistent effect: Udyr's attack speed is passively increased. In addition, Udyr splits his attacks into 3 lightning fast hits. On hit effects only trigger off the first hit. Turtle Stance (W) Active: Udyr gains a temporary shield that absorbs damage. Persistent effect: Udyr's attacks cannot crit, but restore health and mana equivalent to a percentage of damage dealt. Bear Stance (E) Active: Udyr gains a large movement speed increase for a short duration. Persistent effect: Udyr's attacks stun his target for 1 second. This effect cannot occur on the same target for several seconds. Phoenix Stance (R) Active: Udyr sends out pulsing waves of fire dealing damage to nearby foes for 5 seconds.
