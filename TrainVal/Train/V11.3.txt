I Have No Equal Too Bad! League of Legends V11.3 [ ] Client [ ] Rotating Game Mode: ARURF returns to live servers from February 4th, 2021 to March 8th, 2021. Disabled Items: Black Mist Scythe , Bulwark of the Mountain , Frostfang , Harrowing Crescent , Pauldrons of Whiterock , Relic Shield , Runesteel Spaulders , Shard of True Ice , Spectral Sickle , Spellthief's Edge , Steel Shoulderguards , and Targon's Buckler . Enabled Runes: Cosmic Insight , Lethal Tempo , and Ultimate Hunter . Awesome Buff of Awesomely Awesome Buffing Now grants 300 ability haste, item haste and summoner spell haste instead of 80% cooldown reduction on abilities, items and summoner spells. Ability haste you gain from other sources will stack with the URF bonus unlike in previous runs. New Effect: 40% of bonus mana is now converted into bonus health if your champion has mana. New Effect: 100% of bonus mana regeneration is now converted into bonus health regeneration if your champion has mana. Bots New Effect: Bots now build items based off of the recommended items tab. First Win of the Day Undocumented: Reset time increased to 22 hours from 20. Leave Buster New Effect: When a player is flagged as AFK/Leaver at the end of any Ranked game, they will be notified that their LP will be reduced; this applies to both wins and losses. The other players in the lobby will be notified that the flagged AFK/Leaver player will receive less LP over their next several games. If a player would exceed 100 LP, but would fall back below 100 LP after the penalty is applied, they do not promote. League Client Bug Fix: Receiving a game invite will now play a sound if you have another invite pending. Zaun Clash Sign-ups and team formation for the first Clash of Season 2021 will begin during 11.3 on Monday, February 15th. The tournament will officially start in patch 11.4.
