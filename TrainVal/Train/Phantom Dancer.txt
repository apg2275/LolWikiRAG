Strategy Phantom Dancer is used the most efficiently on champions that tend to attack a lot during combat as they can utilize the increased attack speed. Phantom Dancer is best paired with other sources of attack speed in order to stack up the effect quicker. Phantom Dancer's increased movement speed and ghosting allows for ranged users of the item to keep melee enemies at a distance. Phantom Dancer is a useful alternative to Rapid Firecannon in cases where the increased range from the item is not needed, but an increase to attack speed and attack damage is.
