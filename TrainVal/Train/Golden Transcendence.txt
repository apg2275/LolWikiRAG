Golden Transcendence edit Active Blinks your champion to one of the battle platforms. Can only be used from the summoning platform. Grants invulnerability after the blink for 1. 5 seconds. Recipe Cost Sell ID Special No Sell N/A Availability Golden Transcendence was a basic trinket item in League of Legends . It was exclusive to Dominion . Notes The item is part of the Ascension featured game mode during the Shurima event. It was used to get from the spawning fountains to the Crystal Scar 's inner circle, given that a sandstorm raging during the mode prevented foot travel there. Trivia Golden Transcendence could not be used to teleport to the summoning platform. Lore-wise, Golden Transcendence may have been used by Shuriman mages (including Xerath ) to travel across the empire. The item's icon was reused for the in-game Champion Balance Buff tooltip for the 2021 iteration of the One for All Rotating Game Mode .
