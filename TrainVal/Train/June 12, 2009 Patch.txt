If he kills a champion in this time he respawns instantly at base. Lay Waste (Q) A 1-second cooldown spell which leaves a time delayed mine that deals double damage if it only hits a single target. Wall of Pain (W) Stretches a perpendicular wall through the target location, enemies who walk through it are slowed and take increased damage. Defile (E) While toggled on, Defile continually damages nearby enemies. While off, Karthus regains mana whenever he kills a unit.
