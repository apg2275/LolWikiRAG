Similar items Dead Man's Plate Frozen Heart Iceborn Gauntlet Sunfire Aegis Thornmail Unending Despair Jak'Sho, The Protean Stridebreaker Notes Humility is not blocked by spell shield . Humility is an auto-targeted effect. Humility's cast breaks stealth . Humility has no cast time . Humility shoots individual missiles to affected targets to indicate the buff being applied to them. These are not considered projectiles . Critical Resilience's critical strike damage reduction is applied multiplicatively. Critical Resilience's critical strike damage reduction also affects abilities that critically strike, but only those that are treated like a basic attack or deal modified damage. The reduction does not apply to those that deal bonus damage. With Randuin's Omen itself, Rock Solid grants 6. 4 flat damage reduction. Strategy Randuin's Omen is the most effective against champions that deal physical damage with their attacks, especially when they critically strike . Randuin's Omen's active is best used in situations to chase down vulnerable targets or run away from approaching threats. Old icons 1st version 2nd version Trivia Randuin's Omen bears the crest of Demacia's Lightshield Dynasty. Randuin's Omen is named after former Riot Games Inc. employee Robin 'Randuin' Liao . Randuin's Omen's active effect's debuff is named Black Omen, possibly a reference to the floating palace bearing the same name from the game Chrono Trigger . Randuin's Omen is the only item that specifically reduces the amount of damage taken from critical strikes . The item was referenced by the Teamfight Tactics item Randuin's Sanctum .
