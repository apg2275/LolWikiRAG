Notes This enchantment was removed because it didn't allow tank junglers to scale well during the game. It was considered just a stat stick, even though it allowed them to buy a Ninja Tabi without forfeiting Tenacity . In addition, this item also didn't helped tank junglers with their jungle clearing issues. Cinderhulk was designed as a replacement for Juggernaut, fixing the aforementioned issues.
