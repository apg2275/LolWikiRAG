Farsight Orb edit Active Places a visible ward at the target location that grants sight of the surrounding area and lasts 60 seconds. Also grants sight of the area for 2 seconds, with enemy champions inside at the time of placement being revealed for 5 seconds (90 second cooldown; 4000 range). Limitations Limited to 1 Trinket. Selling a Trinket will disable Trinket use for 120 seconds. Requirements Cannot be purchased until level 9 .
