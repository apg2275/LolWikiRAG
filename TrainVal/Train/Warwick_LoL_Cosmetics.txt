His very first splash art was directly tweaked for the first splash art of his Grey skin. Urf the Manatee This skin shares the Urf the Manatee theme with: Urf Kench Urf the Nami-tee Urfrider Corki Urfwick He was released in celebration of April Fools' Day 2010 ( Urf Day ) . He was first priced at 5000 , then went 99% off ( 50 ) and, then settled at 500 , and finally returned to 5000 on the last weekend before becoming Limited Edition (the procedures were donated to a foundation protecting hunted manatees ). He wears Urf's carcass and took over Planet Urf in the background (from the April Fools' Day 2015 login screen). In the old version of his splash art, there was a turret made out of sand in the background. At the grand opening of the Essence Emporium , he received flair effects such as featuring laurels on his head for those who unlocked him on April Fools' Day 2010. This was done to differentiate him with another version of this skin, Urfwick that was released for purchase at the grand opening. Additional details are listed below at his own skin trivia.
