Slow: 15 / 20 / 25 / 30 / 35% Hide Details Info Targeting input Frost Shot is a toggle with an on-hit effect . Attacks made against structures will not consume mana. The slow does not stack with Enchanted Crystal Arrow 's but will with slows from other sources. Frost Shot cannot be toggled on or off while disabled . The cost is per attack , not per hit . While using Runaan's Hurricane , Ashe will not pay triple the mana cost. Likewise, she will still pay the cost even if the basic attack is dodged , blocked , parried , or misses .
