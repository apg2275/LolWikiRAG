He was released in celebration of the Brazilian Server's launch. His Brazilian voiceover indicates his inspiration from The Elite Squad . He might be referencing Che Guevara and/or The Expendables . He resembles John Price from Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare . While on the PBE, Remove Scurvy used to have him eat pineapples and bananas instead of oranges. Cannon Barrage's marked area depicts a Sea Serpent instead of the Bearded Lady . A helicopter can be heard and its lights can be seen for the duration. When recalling , he stands at attention .
