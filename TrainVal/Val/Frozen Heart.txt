Strategy Frozen Heart is best suited for champions that want durability to survive and ability haste and mana to cast their spells. The passives for the aura and damage mitigation can also be used as a counter against champions that need to attack to deal damage. Champions that engage into the middle of a fight can spread the aura to all nearby enemies and gain great value out of the attack speed slow. Old icons 1st version 2nd version Last version (Frozen Brain) Trivia The attack speed debuff affects all units. Frozen Heart's old icon is the same icon used to represent a Citrine gem in the popular MMO World of Warcraft . In that game, the gem appears orange. The item's description "Warning: Do not thaw!" is a reference to the item Eye of Skadi from the mod DotA: Allstars, whose description contains exactly the same line. Frozen Brain was a seasonal item for the 2010 Harrowing celebration that temporarily replaced Frozen Heart . It had the same stats and effect as Frozen Heart , just with a different name and appearance. Pentakill's second volume, Pentakill II: Grasp of the Undying , named its eighth track after this item.
