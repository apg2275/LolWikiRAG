If the user of this item finishes the match with a good performance, there is a small chance an automated message is sent in the game chat with them being killed shortly after. The message reads: "Jak'Sho deems (user's summoner name) worthy and has absorbed their power!!" This death will self-claim shut down bounties, but does not count towards the death score count of KDA .
