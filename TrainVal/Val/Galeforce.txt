Cloudburst deals persistent damage , and thus will trigger spell effects . Cloudburst can deal a maximum of 225 − 525 (based on level) (+ 67. 5 % bonus AD) physical damage . Each of Cloudburst's missiles are blocked by spell shield . Cloudburst is a location-targeted effect. Since the active can be cast at up to 425 range, the missiles can fire at a target within 325 range of the cast location. Cloudburst's cast breaks stealth . Cloudburst has no cast time . Cloudburst will fire at the target regardless of if they are visible or not. Cloudburst's missiles are projectiles with fixed travel times; The first missile is fired after 0. 066 seconds and hits after a combined 0. 33 seconds. The second missile is fired after 0. 198 seconds and hits after a combined 0. 429 seconds. The third missile is fired after 0. 33 seconds and hits after a combined 0. 528 seconds. The missiles arc onto the target in a spline movement. Cloudburst is disabled while grounded or rooted . Naturally, Cloudburst's dash is interrupted by knockdown and airborne effects. Cloudburst renders the user unable to act while dashing. Cloudburst cannot dash through player-generated terrain . Cloudburst will cast at max range if cast beyond that.
