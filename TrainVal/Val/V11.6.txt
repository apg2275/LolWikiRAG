Game [ ] DX11 Last year, we published an article that talked about the upcoming change and rollout for DX11, and it's finally here! In case of issues during the rollout, we've given players the option to fallback to DX9, which can be found in the League Client settings > Game Options > Prefer DX9 Legacy Mode.
