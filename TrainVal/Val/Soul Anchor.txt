Soul Anchor edit Active Consumes a charge to instantly revive you at your summoner platform and grant 125% bonus movement speed that decays over 12 seconds. Starts with 1 charge, with additional charges being granted at levels 9 and 14, and up to 2 charges at once. Limitations Limited to 1 Trinket. Recipe Cost Sell ID Special No Sell 3345 Availability Menu Tools>Vision & Trinkets Soul Anchor was a basic trinket item in League of Legends . It was exclusive to Dominion . Notes Soul Anchor reviving a champion. Trivia The Soul Anchor uses the same animation as Revive upon reviving a champion.
