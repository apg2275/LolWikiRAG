Gromp's basic attacks deal magic damage . Notes [ ] Without slows, the Gromp has a movement speed of 330. When its patience runs out its movement speed is increased by 20%, to 396. Gromp deals 5% of the target's current health bonus physical damage with its attacks. Gromp's attacks are parried by dodge , block , and blind . Trivia [ ] Gromp is a replacement for the Wight on Summoner's Rift since patch V4.20 . Gromp's full name is Lord Grompulus Kevin Ribbiton of Croaksworth . Its animations were recycled for the Dozermaw and Hogtongue monsters from Odyssey: Extraction , a rotating game mode , and Nomsy from mid-set expansion 's Dragonlands of the Teamfight Tactics game.
