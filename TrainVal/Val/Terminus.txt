Terminus edit Stats +40 attack damage +30% attack speed Passive Unique – Shadow : Basic attacks deal 30 bonus magic damage on-hit . Unique – Juxtaposition : Basic attacks on-hit against champions alternate between Light and Dark hits, each one granting a bonus for 5 seconds that stacks up to 5 times. Light hits grant 3 − 5 (based on level) bonus armor and bonus magic resistance while Dark hits grant 6% armor penetration and magic penetration , for a total of 15 − 25 (based on level) bonus resistances and 30% resistances penetration at maximum stacks of each. Limitations Limited to 1 Fatality item. Limited to 1 Blight item.
