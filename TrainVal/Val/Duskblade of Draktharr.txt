Nightstalker will amplify all damage types, including true damage . Nightstalker will not end prematurely from the first instance of a non-basic attack action that breaks stealth as well as any subsequent actions for 0. 25 seconds after that. Nightstalker can activate on clones . The user and their clone both cannot trigger Nightstalker from a single takedown . The clone will only trigger Nightstalker by itself if it scored the killing blow. Nightstalker will not activate if the user is channeling . Nightstalker will not activate from scoring a takedown on a clone . Upon being triggered, Nightstalker's untargetability destroys all incoming hostile non- turret projectiles . Map-Specific Differences Arena differences edit Lethality changed to 22. Nightstalker damage amplifier changed to ( 0% − 25% / 0% − 15% ) (based on target's missing health) . Mythic passive ability haste changed to 7. Old icons 1st version Trivia On release, Duskblade of Draktharr used to apply a mark via basic attack and after a few seconds, would detonate to deal damage based on missing health in addition to its base damage. This was strikingly similar to Zed's Death Mark .
