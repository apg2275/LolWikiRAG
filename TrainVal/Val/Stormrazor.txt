Similar items Phantom Dancer Rapid Firecannon Statikk Shiv Kraken Slayer Runaan's Hurricane Notes Each basic attack on-attack generates 6 Energize stacks while 1 is generated for every 24 in-game units traveled. This includes but is not limited to normal or special forms of movement, using dashes or blinks , or being displaced to another location. Hitting abilities that apply on-hit effects will also generate 6 stacks. Voltaic Cyclosword's Galvanize increases the generation of Energize stacks by 75% for dashes and blinks as well as while moving in stealth . This means it grants 1. 75 stacks for every 24 units travelled during those effects. Bolt deals proc damage , and thus will not trigger spell effects . Bolt is not blocked by spell shield . Stomrazor is an Energized effect and will stack with other sources of it. Energized effects will apply their on-hit damage to structures . Bolt's on-hit damage does not apply to inhibitors nor the Nexus. (bug) Map-Specific Differences Arena differences edit Attack speed changed to 25%. Energized stack per basic attack changed to 12. Energized stack per 24 units travelled changed to 2. Trivia The removed passive Storm's Edge was called Wind Blade in development. Stomrazor is also Yasuo's legendary weapon in Ruined King .
