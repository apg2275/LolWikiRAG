Combat status affects abilities, runes, items and buffs. A champion is in-combat upon damaging or taking damage from an enemy unit (minions, champions, or neutral monsters), otherwise they're out-of-combat . Most out-of-combat effects trigger 5 seconds after being in-combat , resetting each time you're in-combat .
