Abilities Shadow of the Rose Upon encountering a visible enemy champion, this Mimic casts an illusory copy of LeBlanc's selected spell, dealing no damage. After being placed, this Mimic can no longer be controlled. The Mimic copies LeBlanc's appearance to enemies, but remains visually different to LeBlanc and her allies. The Mimic cannot be empowered by Banner of Command's effect Promote . Mimic Abilities After being summoned by LeBlanc's usage of a mimic-empowered ability , this Mimic can be controlled and its basic attacks can deal damage, but do not apply lifesteal . This Mimic's attacks deal only 60% damage to champions and 30% damage to structures. If the attack critically strikes, it applies the standard 2x or 2.5x modifier. Probable Bug: If the Mimic despawns prior to an attack hitting , the attack will scale with total attack damage instead of base . The percentages are otherwise the same. The Mimic's attacks have a seemingly special-cased relationship with on-hit effects : Cannot Apply: Energized , Wind's Fury , Blade of the Ruined King , Guinsoo's Rage . Applies: Wit's End's , Nashor's Tooth's , Phantom Dancer's (benefitting itself), Frozen Mallet's , The Black Cleaver (only 1 stack), Executioner's Calling , Recurve Bow , Guinsoo's Rageblade , Spell Blade (spawns with the effect primed), Hextech Revolver (although deals level 1 damage regardless of level), Elixir of Sorcery (versus structures). The mimic will produce auras, such as Frozen Heart , and benefit from Bramble Vest . These will not stack with LeBlanc's . The mimic's Immolate will deal level 1 damage. LeBlanc and the Mimic can hit different targets with Ethereal Chains . If the Mimic has hit its target with Ethereal Chains it starts to basic attack that target. The Mimic copies LeBlanc's appearance to enemies, but remains visually different to LeBlanc and her allies.
