The active grants 2 seconds of immunity to movement slows only and not attack speed slows . Mikael's Crucible does not cleanse the following crowd control effects: Airborne Suspension will be removed completely. Blind Disarm Only the Root portion of Curse of the Sad Mummy is removed. Only the Polymorph portion of Whimsy is removed. Ground Nearsight Suppression Mikael's Crucible can be self-cast . However, it cannot be activated while under crowd control that denies the activation of abilities (such as being silenced ). Using Mikael's Crucible does not grant assist credit. Mikael's Crucible will also cleanse crowd control effects that apply ~0. 2 seconds after it has been used. This can be used to preemptively cleanse crowd control that denies activation of abilities (such as being silenced ) from yourself if used shortly before the impact. The applied crowd control will still interrupt actions however, such as channels and movement or attack commands. Mikael's Crucible will not remove self slows (except Quinn's Behind Enemy Lines ). The slow immunity will apply to most already-active slows, including self-imposed ones. Note that the slow is not removed and will take effect after the immunity ends (duration permitting).
