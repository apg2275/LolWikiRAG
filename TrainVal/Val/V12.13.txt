Competitive Adding Regional Transfer Rank Preservation. If you transfer your account out of a region and back to that region within two weeks, your account's ranked state upon returning to your original region will be restored to what it was when you left. This is intended to curb some region-transfer rank abuse we've been seeing and also means you won't have to re-do your ranked climb if you take your League account with you on a quick vacation.
