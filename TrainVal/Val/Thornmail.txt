Thorns is not blocked by spell shield . Thorns' reflect is not a projectile . Thorns will trigger regardless of if the basic attack deals damage or not. This includes if the user mitigates all damage with a shield or invulnerability . It will not trigger if the attack is dodged , blocked , or missed while the attacker is blinded . Heals that trigger on-hit will take place after Thorns' Grievous Wounds has been applied. By itself, Thornmail's Thorns reflects at least 22 magic damage ​​ on being hit. Strategy Thornmail is most effective against enemies that use basic attacks to deal damage and attack at a fast rate. It is also useful against enemies that heal through their attacks.
