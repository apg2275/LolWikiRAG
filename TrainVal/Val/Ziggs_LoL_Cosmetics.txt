Mad Scientist Ziggs This skin shares the Mad Scientists theme with: Mad Scientist Singed Sad Robot Amumu Time Machine Zilean He references the eponymous archetype . With the release of the short story Bombs: A Tribute , the skin itself was made canon into the Runeterra Prime universe as how Ziggs looked like during his time the story took place in Zaun.
