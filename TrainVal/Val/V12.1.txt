League of Legends V12.1 [ ] Client [ ] Rotating Game Mode: Ultimate Spellbook returns to live servers from November 18, 2021 (1:00 PM PT) to January 10, 2022 (11:59 PM PT). Forge God Clash In celebration of Ranked Season 2022 Start, there will be a special Forge God Clash tournament with the following special Forge God-themed rewards. 3 Trophies. 3 Banners. 5 Team Logos. Forge God clash will only take place on one weekend. Team formation opens on January 10, with matches taking place on January 15 and 16. Forge God Clash is not part of the regular Clash season. You'll still be able to earn Forge God-themed rewards, but your Clash VP track won't be affected. The first Clash tournament of Season 2022, which will count towards the VP track, will be in patch 12.4. Intentional Feeding Detection Intentional Feeding Detection has been improved to evaluate more variables and will go live to all regions this patch. Ranked Season 2022 The Summoner's Rift Ranked Season 2022 begins on Friday, January 7, 2022. All ranked accounts will receive a soft reset of 5 tiers for initial seeding. For example, Gold I will become Silver II.
