Each ability grants a Stance : a unique passive effect that lasts until casting a different ability. Switching Stances will prevent his previous Stance from triggering any more on-hit effects, and incur a 1. 5 -second global cooldown on his other abilities. Starting at level 16, all abilities can gain a 6th rank. Innate: Udyr's ability casts generate a stack of Monkey's Agility for 5 seconds, refreshing on subsequent casts and stacking up to 3 times. Monkey's Agility: For each stack, Udyr gains 5 bonus movement speed and 10% bonus attack speed , up to 15 bonus movement speed and 30% bonus attack speed . Hide Details Info Targeting input Passive Cloud Dragon Soul's increased movement speed buff is special cased to activate on all of Udyr's spell casts due to not having a traditional ultimate. The global cooldown will not incur on abilities that are currently on a greater cooldown. Udyr does not receive extra skill points for his extra ability ranks, meaning he will not be able to reach maximum rank on all of his abilities unless he can level beyond the normal limit (e.g. Ultra Rapid Fire ). Tiger Stance Tiger Stance COST: 45 − 28 (based on level) Mana COOLDOWN: 6 CAST TIME: none Stance: Udyr's basic attacks on-hit generate a stack of Tiger . At 3 stacks, his next basic attack is empowered to consume them all to inflict Tiger Strike , and also generates a Tiger stack. Tiger Strike: The target is dealt bonus physical damage every 0. 5 seconds over 2 seconds. Leftover damage is dealt instantly upon subsequent infliction. Bonus Attack Speed: 30 / 40 / 50 / 60 / 70 / 80% Hide Details Info Targeting input Auto Damage type Type Sub-type DOT damage Physical Counters Spell shield Parries Not Blocked See Notes The name of the buff granted by entering Tiger Stance is Tiger Speed . The triggering attack will generate the first stack toward the next, meaning the effect will always trigger on every 3rd attack. Guinsoo's Rageblade will reduce this number to every 2nd attack, with Udyr's attack animations coded to "look ahead" and trigger the empowered-attack animation if he attacks a target while at 1 stack while Guinsoo's Rage is active. Tiger Stance's primed damage over time does not persist through stance changes. Unlike most on-hit physical damage, the bonus damage from Tiger Stance will not apply life steal. The on-hit application of the DoT cannot be dodged nor blocked , but it can miss . The empowered attack will not trigger against structures. Turtle Stance Turtle Stance COST: 45 − 28 (based on level) Mana COOLDOWN: 6 CAST TIME: none Stance: Udyr's basic attacks on-hit generate a stack of Turtle . At 3 stacks, his next basic attack is empowered to consume them all to heal himself for 2% − 4% (based on missing health) of his maximum health , and also generates a Turtle stack. This effect does not trigger if Udyr is at full health. Active: Udyr shields himself for up to 5 seconds, and instantly generates 3 Turtle stacks. Shield Strength: 60 / 90 / 120 / 150 / 180 / 210 (+ 45% AP) Hide Details Info Targeting input Auto The name of the buff granted by entering Turtle Stance is Spiritual Shell . The triggering attack will generate the first stack toward the next, meaning the effect will always trigger on every 3rd attack. Guinsoo's Rageblade will reduce this number to every 2nd attack, with Udyr's attack animations coded to "look ahead" and trigger the empowered-attack animation if he attacks a target while at 1 stack while Guinsoo's Rage is active. The empowered attack will not trigger against structures. Bear Stance Bear Stance COST: 45 − 28 (based on level) Mana COOLDOWN: 6 CAST TIME: none TARGET IMMUNITY : 5 Stance: Udyr's basic attacks are empowered to have an uncancellable windup and stun the target for 1 second. The target is then marked Mauled for 5 seconds, becoming immune to the stun. Active: Udyr gains bonus movement speed and ghosting for the next few seconds. During this time, his basic attacks against enemy champions are empowered to lunge 50 units toward them, unless they are marked Mauled . Bonus Movement Speed: 15 / 20 / 25 / 30 / 35 / 40% Buff Duration: 2 / 2. 25 / 2. 5 / 2. 75 / 3 / 3. 25 Hide Details Info Targeting input Auto Counters Spell shield Parries Blocked Blocked The name of the buff granted by entering Bear Stance is Primal Charge . The name of the immunity debuff granted from Bear Stance is Mauled . Bear Stance's self-displacement does not count as a dash for the purposes of Sudden Impact . The triggering attack will apply other on-hit effects and can critically strike as normal. Spell shield and crowd control immunity will consume the attack, meaning the on-target stun immunity is also incurred in the process. The empowered attack will not trigger against structures.
