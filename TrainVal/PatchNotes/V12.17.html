League of Legends V12.17[]

Client[]

Behavioral Systems
  • Over the last 8 months since we released our new feeding detection model, we have gained the confidence that allows us to make significant adjustments to what we detect as intentional feeding. We expect an increase in detections and punishments of over 2x.
  • Chat restrictions will be visible on the lobbies screen for yourself and any party members, as well as on individual profile pages.
  • Honor restrictions applied to your account will be visible on your profile page with a lock icon and tooltip.
Challenges
  • Global Defense System has been added: Have a control ward in the enemy jungle for 65+% of the game.
  • Bug Fix: Executing to the Fountain or minions no longer progresses the "Free Ticket to Base" challenge.
  • Bug Fix: Sion no longer counts as an additional kill or assist for certain challenges when he dies during Glory in Death.
Clash
  • Team formation for the second weekend of the Noxus Cup starts on September 12, and the tournament days are on September 17 and 18.
Competitive
  • Summoner's Rift Ranked queue matchmaking will now focus more on player's MMR and less on their visible rank (e.g. Silver or Gold).

Game[]

Cursor
  • Bug Fix: Cursor no longer moves erratically when using smart pings based on distance from the center of the screen.
Tooltips
  • Bug Fix: Updated tooltips to accurately display the correct values for champions that can switch between melee and ranged forms. They now dynamically update their values based on which form they are currently in.
