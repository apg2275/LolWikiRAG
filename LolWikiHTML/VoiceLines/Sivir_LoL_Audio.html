Trivia[]

Sivir, the Battle Mistress
  • ▶️  "Everyone has a price" might be referencing Ted DiBiase.
  • Sivir shares quotes with:
    • ▶️   ▶️   "Catch!"
    • ▶️   ▶️   "Heads up!"
      • The lines in Ekko's voice-over might be deliberate copies (as his released well after Sivir's), since both abilities feature a returning weapon.
Solar Eclipse Sivir
  • The music playing during her dance animation resembles that of Solar Eclipse Leona / Lunar Eclipse Leona. If timed perfectly, they create a mesmerizing melody together.
    • The score can be listened to here.
  • Some of her lines are altered from their counterpart in her base skin:
  • ▶️  "Every god has its bounty." refers to ▶️  "Everyone has a price."
  • ▶️  "Gods come and go, but gold remains." refers to ▶️  "Kings come and go, but gold stays."
  • ▶️  "Thou should duck!" refers to ▶️  "Better duck!"
