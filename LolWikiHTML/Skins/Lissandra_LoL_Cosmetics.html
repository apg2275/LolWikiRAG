Trivia[]

Original Lissandra
  • The Howling Abyss bridge can be seen in the background.
  • She resembles Elesh Norn from Magic: The Gathering.[1]
  • She might be referencing Akasha from Unreal Tournament 3.
  • She resembles a Type-S Nova from the manwha and anime series Freezing.
Bloodstone Lissandra
  • This skin shares the Bloodstone theme with:
    • Blood Knight Hecarim
    • Blood Lord Vladimir
    • Bloodfury Renekton
    • Bloodstone Taric
    • Dark Crystal Ryze
    • Nightraven Fiora
  • She references Marrow Shards from Magic: The Gathering.
Blade Queen Lissandra
  • This skin shares the Omen of the Dark theme with:
    • Black Scourge Singed
    • Cursed Revenant Nocturne
    • Executioner Mundo
    • Grey Warwick
    • Iron Inquisitor Kayle
    • Spectral Fiddlesticks
    • Undertaker Yorick
  • She might have been inspired by Hexadecimal from ReBoot.
  • She resembles the Lady of Pain from Dungeons & Dragons.
  • Frozen Tomb becomes an Iron maiden (she encases herself in it when recalling).
  • Glacial Path's claw resembles the Ezrohir Artificer's from Torchlight II.
  • Her name might be refencing Sarah Kerrigan from StarCraft ('Queen of Blades').
  • She resembles the The Arisen from Allods Online.
Program Lissandra
  • This skin shares the Program theme with:
    • iBlitzcrank
    • Program Camille
    • Program LeBlanc
    • Program Nami
    • Program Soraka
  • Multiple iBlitzcrank units can be seen in the background.
  • A hologram of Program Soraka can be seen in the foreground.
  • She was released alongside Program Soraka.
  • She might have been inspired by GLaDOS from Portal and the Cell from Transistor.
  • In Brazilian localization, she (along with all Program skins) is called Lissandra Cibernética (Cyber Lissandra) due to Program's translated double meaning.
  • The skin was originally pitched as 'sci fi Lissandra skin' and Sunny 'Kindlejack' Pandita was encouraged to explore a wide range (including PROJECT and a Cyberpop pitch).[2]
Coven Lissandra
Chromas: Aquamarine, Dark Ritual, Meteorite, Obsidian, Rose Quartz, Ruby, Sandstone, Tanzanite
  • This skin shares the Coven theme with:
    • Coven Akali
    • Coven Camille
    • Coven Evelynn
    • Coven LeBlanc
    • Coven Zyra
    • ...and others
    • Old God Ivern
    • Old God Warwick
    • Prestige Coven Akali
    • Prestige Coven LeBlanc
    • Prestige Coven Zyra
  • She was released alongside Coven Camille.
  • Similar to the other Coven skin, she has an animal headpiece. Lissandra's headpiece is similar to stag antlers.
    • This, among other reasons, has led to speculation that Old God Nocturne (who has stag-like antlers) may be the Ivory Stag patron mentioned in her skin bio, though this has not been confirmed.
  • She can disappear in magical ravens, as seen in her Recall, Glacial Path and death animation.
    • She might be referencing Ravenna from Snow White and the Huntsmen.
Dark Cosmic Lissandra
Chromas: Citrine, Emerald, Obsidian, Pearl, Rainbow, Rose Quartz, Ruby, Sapphire, Turquoise
  • This skin shares the Dark Star theme with:
    • Dark Cosmic Jhin
    • Dark Cosmic Lux
    • Dark Cosmic Erasure Jhin
    • Dark Star Cho'Gath
    • Dark Star Karma
    • ...and others
    • Dark Star Orianna
    • Dark Star Shaco
    • Dark Star Thresh
    • Dark Star Varus
    • Dark Star Xerath
  • This skin might be a reference or inspired by the Goddess from the The Dream Thief short story.
  • The skin was inspired by the Ara constellation.[3]
Porcelain Lissandra
Chromas: Amethyst, Aquamarine, Catseye, Emerald, Golden Tiger, Obsidian, Pearl, Rose Quartz, Ruby
  • This skin shares the Porcelain theme with:
    • Porcelain Amumu
    • Porcelain Darius
    • Porcelain Graves
    • Porcelain Kindred
    • Porcelain Lux
    • ...and others
    • Porcelain Miss Fortune
    • Porcelain Protector Ezreal
    • Prestige Porcelain Kindred
    • Prestige Porcelain Lissandra
    • Prestige Porcelain Lux
Space Groove Lissandra
Chromas: Catseye, Emerald, Obsidian, Pearl, Rainbow, Rose Quartz, Ruby, Sapphire
  • This skin shares the Space Groove theme with:
    • Prestige Space Groove Lulu
    • Space Groove Blitz & Crank
    • Space Groove Gragas
    • Space Groove Gwen
    • Space Groove Lulu
    • ...and others
    • Space Groove Lux
    • Space Groove Nasus
    • Space Groove Nunu & Willump
    • Space Groove Samira
    • Space Groove Teemo
  • Blitz & Crank (without their mech) are featured in her splash art.
    • Space Groove Teemo can be seen in the background.
  • Before her release, she was previously mentioned in the skin bios of Space Groove Nasus, Space Groove Nunu & Willump, Space Groove Rumble, and Space Groove Samira.
