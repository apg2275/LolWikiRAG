Trivia[]

Original Nunu & Willump
  • Nunu has brown eyes in his artwork, while his eyes are green in game.
    • His eyes are also brown in Grungy Nunu & Willump's artwork.
  • His very first splash art was directly tweaked for the first splash art of his Sasquatch skin.
    • The second splash art for his Sasquatch skin also seems to have borrowed the pose from his first classic splash art.
Sasquatch Nunu & Willump
  • This skin shares the Fables theme with:
    • Dark Valkyrie Diana
    • Djinn Malzahar
    • Loch Ness Cho'Gath
    • Muse Sona
    • Musketeer Twisted Fate
    • ...and others
    • Mythic Cassiopeia
    • Perseus Pantheon
    • Royal Guard Fiora
    • Siren Cassiopeia
    • Valkyrie Leona
  • Willump references the Sasquatch, another name for Bigfoot.
  • Sasquatch Nunu
    • Willump wears the Banshee's Veil around his neck.
Workshop Nunu & Willump
  • This skin shares the Snowdown Showdown theme with:
    • Candy Cane Miss Fortune
    • Festive Maokai
    • Happy Elf Teemo
    • Mistletoe LeBlanc
    • Nutcracko
    • ...and others
    • Re-Gifted Amumu
    • Santa Braum
    • Santa Draven
    • Santa Gragas
    • Snow Bunny Nidalee
  • Nunu is dressed like a Christmas elf from Santa's workshop while Willump is a Giant Reindeer Poro.
  • Svellsongur is replaced with a candy cane.
  • He was released in celebration of Snowdown Showdown 2009 along with:
    • Snow Bunny Nidalee
    • Happy Elf Teemo
    • Earnest Elf Tristana
    • Old Saint Zilean
  • Workshop Nunu
    • Nunu is dressed like a Christmas elf from Santa's workshop while Willump is dressed like Santa Claus.
Grungy Nunu & Willump
  • This skin shares the Toy Box theme with:
    • Dino Gnar
    • Moo Cow Alistar
    • Pug'Maw
    • Ragdoll Poppy
    • Renektoy
    • Sewn Chaos Orianna
    • Toy Soldier Gangplank
  • Renektoy and toy versions of Tibbers, Original Teemo, Cottontail Teemo and Grungy Willump can be seen in the background.
  • Nunu references Boo while Willump references Sulley (both of which are characters from the animated Disney Pixar film, Monsters, Inc.).
  • Svellsongur is replaced with a toy recorder.
  • Grungy Nunu
    • Nunu is a reference to Oscar the Grouch while Willump is a reference to the Cookie Monster, both from Sesame Street.
Nunu & Willump Bot
  • This skin shares the Heavy Metal theme with:
    • Full Machine Viktor
    • Full Metal Jayce
    • Full Metal Pantheon
    • Full Metal Rammus
  • The background of their splash art is the same arena as in Full Metal Pantheon splash art.
  • "WILLUMP" is an acronym for Workframe Integrated Lossless Lifeform Uploaded Mainly for Punching.
  • Svellsongur is replaced with a wrench secured to Nunu's back.
    • Nunu fires energy blasts from his wrench during Snowball Barrage.
  • Their joke creates a holographic projection of old Nunu Bot.
  • Their dance is likely a reference to the "Disco Nunu" meme, where one would gamethrow by dancing in the middle of a lane in order to get killed by the enemy while using Clairvoyance and Scrying Orb to give the appearance of a disco ball.
  • Nunu Bot
    • He was named after and references the eponymous series by 'angrygoran'.
      • His voice differs from the series because, according to 'angrygoran' himself, another one had to be used due to copyright concerns.
Demolisher Nunu & Willump
  • This skin shares the Rift Quest theme with:
    • Bard Bard
    • Braum Lionheart
    • Gragas Caskbreaker
    • Lord Darius
    • Rageborn Mundo
    • ...and others
    • Rugged Garen
    • Ryze Whitebeard
    • Sejuani Dawnchaser
    • Twitch Shadowfoot
    • Varus Swiftbolt
  • Svellsongur is replaced with a spiked mace that resembles pre-rework Mordekaiser's mace.
  • Demolisher Nunu
    • He references Master Blaster from Mad Max Beyond Thunderdome and/or Juggernaut from X-Men by Marvel Comics.
TPA Nunu & Willump
  • This skin shares the World Champions: 2012 theme with:
    • TPA Ezreal
    • TPA Orianna
    • TPA Shen
    • TPA Mundo
  • He was released in celebration of Taipei Assassins winning the Season Two World Championship along with:
    • TPA Mundo
    • TPA Ezreal
    • TPA Orianna
    • TPA Shen
  • He represents MiSTakE.
Zombie Nunu & Willump
  • This skin shares the Zombies VS Slayers theme with:
    • Zombie Brand
    • Zombie Ryze
    • Zombie Slayer Jinx
    • Zombie Slayer Pantheon
  • Zombie Brand can be seen in the background.
  • He was released in celebration of Harrowing 2015 along with:
    • Zombie Slayer Jinx
    • Zombie Slayer Pantheon
  • They reference The Walking Dead, Left 4 Dead, and/or Zombie Tycoon.
  • All ice effects are replaced with a substance resembling vomit and/or radioactive liquid.
  • Both Nunu and Willump groan to each other instead of speaking.
Papercraft Nunu & Willump
Chromas: Amethyst, Aquamarine, Catseye, Obsidian, Pearl, Rose Quartz, Ruby
  • This skin shares the Papercraft theme with:
    • Papercraft Anivia
  • While not directly sharing a splash art with Papercraft Anivia, their splash arts can join together to make one larger image.
  • This skin was inspired by Origami.
  • Svellsongur is replaced with a pair of scissors secured to Nunu's back. During his dance, Nunu instead blows through a makeshift paper flute.
  • The paper prop in Nunu & Willump's recall is actually 4 different models swapping in/out.[1]
Space Groove Nunu & Willump
Chromas: Catseye, Disco, Emerald, Obsidian, Pearl, Rose Quartz, Ruby, Sapphire, Turquoise
  • This skin shares the Space Groove theme with:
    • Prestige Space Groove Lulu
    • Space Groove Blitz & Crank
    • Space Groove Gragas
    • Space Groove Gwen
    • Space Groove Lissandra
    • ...and others
    • Space Groove Lulu
    • Space Groove Lux
    • Space Groove Nasus
    • Space Groove Samira
    • Space Groove Teemo
Nunu & Beelump
Chromas: Emerald, Obsidian, Pearl, Rainbow, Rose Quartz, Ruby, Sapphire, Tanzanite
  • This skin shares the Bees! theme with:
    • Bee'Koz
    • Bee'Maw
    • Beekeeper Singed
    • Beemo
    • Beezahar
    • ...and others
    • Beezcrank
    • BZZZiggs
    • King Beegar
    • Orbeeanna
    • Yuubee
  • Svellsongur is replaced with a daisy flower with a smiley face.
Cosmic Paladins Nunu & Willump
Chromas: Amethyst, Catseye, Emerald, Obsidian, Pearl, Rose Quartz, Ruby, Turquoise
  • This skin shares the Cosmic theme with:
    • Cosmic Lux
    • Cosmic Charger Hecarim
    • Cosmic Defender Xin Zhao
    • Cosmic Enchantress Lulu
    • Cosmic Huntress Nidalee
    • ...and others
    • Cosmic Invoker Illaoi
    • Cosmic Matriarch Bel'Veth
    • Cosmic Paladin Nautilus
    • Cosmic Reaver Kassadin
    • Cosmic Sting Skarner
