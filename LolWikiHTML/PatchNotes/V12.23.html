League of Legends V12.23[]

Client[]

Behavioral Systems
  • End of Season Honor rewards including the new Honor level 5 Malzahar skin ( Three Honors Malzahar) reward will be granted according to your Honor level at the End of Season.
Champion Select
  • Anonymity: Ally 1 Ally 5 have been renamed to Gromp, Murk Wolf, Raptor, Krug, and Scuttle Crab for improved clarity around who is speaking in champion select chat.
  • In champion select, Sort by Favorites is the default sorting option. Note that the fallback option is Sort by Name so a player who does not have any favorites selected will still have the same experience.
    • A player's selection for Sort by preference is saved between champion selects.
Clash
  • Bug Fix: Clash capsules, orbs, banners, and trophies are now properly awarded.
Mythic Content
  • In:
    • Prestige Arcade Caitlyn
    • Prestige PROJECT: Sylas
    • Hextech Rammus
    • Hextech Kassadin
    • Ashen Graveknight Mordekaiser, Mythic Chroma, Icon (Emberwoken), and Emote
    • Anima Squad 2022 Grab Bag
    • Star Guardian 2022 Grab Bag
    • Anima Squad 2022 Mythic Essence Icon
    • Star Guardian 2022 Mythic Essence Icon
    • MSI High Noon 2022 Mythic Essence Icon
    • Steel Valkyrie 2022 Mythic Essence Icon
    • Worlds 2022 Mythic Essence Icon
    • Eclipse Knights 2022 Mythic Essence Icon
    • Ocean Song 2022 Mythic Essence Icon
  • Out:
    • Prestige Star Guardian Neeko
    • Prestige Conqueror Jax
Pick Order Swapping
  • In matchmade queues with drafting, during the declaration, ban, and pick phases, you can now press a button next to ally portraits to swap pick order with them. When there is 5 seconds left in a phase, in-progress order swap requests will be canceled. If your order swap request is declined, you cannot initiate another request with that player.
  • Champion swapping during the preparation phase is now initiated by clicking on the allied champion's portrait instead of a button next to the portrait.
  • Miscellaneous champion select font and text changes have been made for consistency.
Rune Recommender
  • Added the Rune Recommender to champion select for all game modes. These recommendations are tailored for your champion, position, and come with optional summoner spell recommendations as well. Like in-game item recommendations, these will be updated each patch.
  • All players have a free temporary rune page that the recommender will overwrite in each champion select. To permanently save a recommendation to your collection, you can add it in the post match screen.
  • The 5 preset rune pages have been removed.

Game[]

Announcer
  • Bug Fix: Announcer will now properly play the voice line that alerts Turret Plating will soon fall.
Objective Planning
  • Scoreboard voting has now been disabled. Clicking on the objective timer will no longer initiate objective votes and will instead just display the timer information in chat.
  • Vote timing has been adjusted so the earliest time players can start voting for an objective from 90 seconds before an objective spawns to 75 seconds.
  • Auto vote has been disabled. The vote to contest an objective will no longer automatically begin 30 seconds before an objective spawns.
Pings
  • Ping sizes have been slightly reduced.
  • Improved clarity of the Push ping VFX.
  • Polished the ping ripple VFX and visual fidelity of the icons.
Vision updates
  • Bug Fix: Certain champions (e.g. Kindred) will no longer sometimes briefly appear on the player's screen for a frame when they exit the Fog of War somewhere else on the map.
    • This was reflected on the mini-map as well for the icons of champions.

Burst Fire is empowered to have a 20% shorter cast time and instead fire 3 rounds that travel with increased speed. These attacks chain to the nearest visible enemy within 650-units of the target, up to 4 subsequent targets, dealing 25% AD physical damage, which can critically strike for (18.75% +  10%) AD bonus physical damage.

Secondary targets are dealt 25% AD physical damage, which can critically strike for (18.75% +  10%) AD bonus physical damage. The bonus magic damage is affected by critical strike modifiers.
      • Additionally, Zeri generates stacks of Overcharge from hitting enemy champions, gaining 0.5% bonus movement speed per stack. She generates 1 stack for each one she hits with a charged basic attack or ability, increased to 3 on abilities that critically strike and 8 on Lightning Crash. Burst Fire grants stacks only against the first target hit. Overcharge's duration is extended by 2 seconds whenever Zeri generates a stack, up to its original duration.

Howling Abyss[]

ARAM Clash
  • The last Clash of the year will now be held on the Howling Abyss.
  • This Clash is free to enter and features an 8-team bracket where each team gets to play 3 matches. Each player will receive 2 rerolls per game.
  • Registration for this special Clash event begins on December 5th, 2022. Tournament days will be on December 10th and 11th, 2022.
ARAM-specific balancing
  • Damage reduction increased to 15% − 30% (based on distance) from 15% at all distances over 1000 units.
  • Heartsteel
    • Bonus health to damage conversion reduced to 5% from 10%.
Brush
  • There is now a pixel brush in the middle of the map to the bottom side of the bridge.
Champion Select
  • Players can now see who purchased a Battle Boost.
    • Battle Boosts can no longer be purchased with 10 seconds remaining.
  • Players are now able to see which champions will grant them a Hextech Chest if they achieve an S rank on said champion.
Death timers
  • Death timers reduced to 12 − 43 (based on level) seconds from 10 − 40 (based on level).
Frostgates - New map feature
  • There are now frost-gates in front of each team's spawn, which function the same as those on the Hextech Rift map on Summoner's Rift. Champions can interact with these gates to teleport to the front of their outer turret.
    • Leading gates cannot be interacted with.
    • If the outer turret is destroyed, the leading gate is relocated to the front of the inhibitor turret.
    • Frostgates on both sides of the map can be interacted with by champions regardless of which team they're on.
Homestart
  • Removed: Before 5 minutes or until the first turret is destroyed, champions no longer gain 50% bonus movement speed for 10 seconds after spawning or respawning.
Howling Abyss Aura
  • New Effect: Omnivamp and life steal against minions are now reduced by 50%.
  • Bonus magic resistance for melee champions increased to 15 from 10.
Remake
  • Idle length detection increased to 120 seconds from 90.
Terrain
  • The gaps on the bottom side of the bridge have been filled in and are now traversable.
Turrets
  • New Effect: Whenever an outer or inner turret is destroyed, it now falls in a direction and its ruins remain as terrain. All turrets fall in the same direction. They can either fall upwards or downwards, randomly determined each game.

Practice Tool[]

  • Bug Fix: The rift no longer only transforms into the Infernal Rift after two drakes are slain.
