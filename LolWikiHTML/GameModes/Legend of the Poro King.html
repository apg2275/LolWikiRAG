Legend of the Poro King

Legend of the Poro King is a temporary game mode released for the Snowdown Showdown event. Its first appearance was in 2014. Players fight on the Howling Abyss, with the goal is still to destroy the enemy nexus. Beside that, players try to summon the Poro King to aid sieging.

The mode was released with either All Random or Blind pick drafting.

Gameplay[]

Summoner spells[]

When playing the Legend of the Poro King, your regular summoner spells are replaced with two new ones exclusive to this mode, Poro Toss / Poro Dash and To the King!. Unsealed Spellbook does not allow access to regular summoner spells.

Poro King[]

Main article: Poro King

Poro King

The Poro King is benevolent and will aid whichever team has summoned him. He can be dispelled, but only to return again when enough favor has been curried with him. As a tanky siege minion, he brings an impressive power boost to whichever team he's aiding.

Players can also feed him with Snax to trigger his special abilities.

New Items[]

  • Frosted Snax - Shoots a barrage of ice missiles. So cold!
  • Super Spicy Snax - This is too hot for Poros, and causes the King to spew fire! Fire is hot... don't touch that!
  • Espresso Snax - So... much... caffeine! O_O The King bounces around, knocking back enemies and gaining a shield.
  • Rainbow Snax Party Pack! - Snacks for all Poros! Whee! The Poros get so excited for the Snax that they stampede out, pulling any champions in their path.


Balance Changes[]

All Howling Abyss specific balance changes apply.


Trivia[]

  • While playing this mode, each champion is followed by two poros. These poros change appearance when using one of the new poro summoner icons.[1] For the list of icons, go to Media/Icons tab.
  • The summoner spell Poro Toss/ Poro Dash was later implemented on the ARAM mode. The ARAM version spells are called Mark and Dash.
